## Session Analysis

### Narrative Summary

Session 11 picks up immediately after the Feydark forced long rest and level-up from S10. The session is structured in two distinct acts: Act 1 consists of individualized dream/trance sequences for each PC where they receive empowered trinkets and personal gifts from [[Psilofyr]] (the Carrion King); Act 2 reunites the party for the Nexus Pools choice and departure from the Feydark.

**Act 1 — Individual Dream Sequences:**

[[Kit Aluri]] awakens alone on a bed of dry moss, surrounded by fog. She is weaponless and surrounded by an army of undead soldiers. Kit attempts to fight — rolling a contested Dex check (nat 1, saved to 9 via Tactical Mind, still fails vs. soldier's 17), taking 3 slashing damage. She attempts unarmed strikes (10 miss, then Riposte at 20 hit for 1 damage, then 11 miss with superiority die boost to 12 still misses). Six soldiers multi-attack: four hit for 26 slashing damage total. Kit is reduced to 6 HP, then goes down on the next round. Relentless Endurance does NOT activate (DM narration: "it does not happen"). Kit makes a death save — nat 20. As she loses consciousness, her Doll of the Fallen Soldier animates, climbs out of her pocket, grows to medium size, and smiles down at her. Kit's internal monologue is one of acceptance — she believes this is karma, retribution for horrible things she did as a soldier. The doll's original owner appears to be forgiving her. Kit receives the **Doll of the Fallen Soldier** (+1 CHA; on a failed death save, the doll animates to protect Kit or a chosen ally, causing disadvantage on attacks against the target and carrying them from danger).

[[Aeolus]] awakens and sees [[Naos]] (his Archfey patron) sitting on a mossy stone, fiddling with a wedding band. Naos delivers a lengthy exposition about his motives: he fell in love with Aeolus on the material plane, bonded them together to gain power, but did so without Aeolus's knowledge or consent. He learned of the threat from the Carrion King — a corrupted counterpart of Psilofyr who emerged from the Abyss and seeks to control death itself. Naos attempted to infiltrate the threat alone but failed and is now in danger, needing rescue. He kneels and offers the wedding ring to Aeolus. Aeolus takes it. Naos's essence floods into the ring and he dissolves into spores. [[Echo]] lands and pecks at the ring: "So dramatic. What a romantic." Aeolus receives the **Ring of Spell Storing** (+1 WIS).

[[Blarg]] awakens among the fae goblins from S10. Their leader, **Chris**, speaks to Blarg in Goblin. Chris recognizes Blarg's goblin scent and the family carving. Chris reveals he knew Blarg's adoptive goblin shaman **Carbra** from inter-tribal gatherings. Chris offers to empower Blarg's family carving with goblin fae magic. The carving transforms — the ears of the carved family grow into goblinoid ears, gaining a jade-green hue. The carving can shift between dwarven and goblin family forms at will. Blarg receives +1 CHA, goblin wild shape (keeps own stats but gains goblin appearance and Fury of the Small), and permanent welcome among goblin tribes.

[[Amanita de'Champignon]] enters a deep elven trance. Her consciousness sinks through the mycelial network to the center — a glowing light that IS Psilofyr. Psilofyr warns of the corrupted counterpart entity (female, from the Abyss, seeks to control death/rot) and shows Amanita visions of four possible future forms she could choose: arch druid, mycelial shield-bearer, barbaric myconid sovereign, or direct Carrion King patron champion. Psilofyr empowers Amanita's **pipe** and **warpick**: the warpick becomes an enspelled weapon with Earth Tremor and Shillelagh (can be cast on the warpick specifically, DM homebrew). Amanita gains +1 INT and rapport spores ability. Psilofyr then shifts into a casual, bro-like persona — mushroom wizard cap, saying "dude" and "bummer." They fist-bump. Amanita's rapport spores are upgraded: 30ft emanation to start, then unlimited range on the same plane for the full hour duration.

[[Darby Stonefeather]]'s trinket empowerment happens during the "morning" transition. Her carved totem shifts between her father's face and an axe beak. She receives +1 CHA and a fourth rage option: **Axe Beak** (movement increases to 50ft; all attacks add an extra D4 slashing damage on hit).

[[Binks Stonevein]] tries to go back to sleep, believing he's dead. While dozing, he attunes to his loot pick and hears his grandfather's voice: "A wise Goliath once told me that your strength comes from your friends." Binks gains +1 STR and his loot pick gains **Ring of Mind Shielding** properties (immune to thought-reading magic, lie detection, alignment/creature type detection; telepathy only by permission).

**Act 2 — Reunion, Topsy's Resurrection, and the Nexus Pools:**

[[Turvy]] leads [[Binks Stonevein]] to a zurkhwood tent where [[Topsy]] lies alive, wrapped in mycelial fibers. The Carrion King retrieved her body from the sealed safe house and resurrected her. Binks makes a medicine check (nat 24) and creates a healing salve from local fungi to strengthen Topsy's recovery. Kit's reaction to seeing Topsy alive is the emotional peak of the session — she falls to her knees sobbing, processing the guilt of killing Topsy for the first time since it happened.

[[Darby Stonefeather]] wakes early and wanders the camp, observing sleeping party members (including standing over Kit, who wakes up and instinctively punches Darby — unarmed strike with disadvantage, 5 and 6, DM rules it as a shin hit, no real damage).

[[Blarg]] wild shapes into goblin form to hang out with Chris's band. Aeolus catches him (insight nat 20) and takes it in stride.

Amanita activates her upgraded rapport spores, hotboxing the zurkhwood tent and connecting the entire party telepathically. DM rules the range upgrade: 30ft emanation initially, then unlimited range on the same plane for the duration. Three uses per day, one hour each.

[[Psilofyr]] leads the party to the **Nexus Pools** — a glade of autumn trees with circular pools, each showing a different destination via watery mirrors:
- [[Velkynvelve]] (Topsy/Turvy's pool)
- [[Mantol-Derith]] (Kit's pool)
- [[04-World-Lore/Locations/Blingdenstone]] (Binks's pool)
- [[04-World-Lore/Locations/Neverlight Grove]] (Amanita's pool)
- [[Sloobludop]] (Blarg's pool)
- [[Gauntlgrym]] (Eldeth's pool)
- The Surface — a mountain pass (Darby's pool)

Psilofyr grants the party the **Fey Touched feat** (burning roots wrap around each PC's arms).

[[Eldeth]] departs through the Gauntlgrym portal to warn the dwarven kings and bring help. Emotional farewell.

The party debates and chooses **Velkynvelve** — Binks wants to settle a score, Amanita thinks they can take them now, Blarg wants to save prisoners. Kit defers to majority rule. DM rolls a d20 for the time of arrival: **10** — the party will arrive after the demon battle but while the outpost is still defended and on guard.

Roxanne's appearance has changed: her body is now a mixture of natural rothe and fungal material. The chest has transformed into two zurkhwood saddles on either side, allowing half the party to ride comfortably.

Session ends as the party steps through the Velkynvelve portal.

### Themes & Emotional Beats

- **Karmic Acceptance:** Kit's nightmare is the emotional core of this session. She doesn't fight the death — she accepts it as deserved punishment for her past as a soldier. The doll's animation represents forgiveness she doesn't believe she deserves, making it the most psychologically complex moment in the campaign.
- **Patron Relationships:** Aeolus's scene with Naos reframes the entire warlock-patron dynamic. Naos admits manipulation but also genuine love. The ring represents both a romantic and magical bond. Aeolus accepts without anger — growth from the kill-on-sight attitude of S10.
- **Found Family:** Blarg's goblin connection expands his identity. He is both dwarf and goblin, adopted twice over. Chris's recognition of Carbra bridges Blarg's past and present. The carving that shifts between families is a physical metaphor.
- **The Champion's Choice:** Amanita's four possible futures are the first time the DM has explicitly shown class/path branching options. This is both narrative foreshadowing and mechanical signposting.
- **Resurrection and Guilt:** Kit's breakdown over Topsy's resurrection is the payoff for five sessions of guilt. She killed Topsy in S04, advocated for her resurrection in S09, and now sees the result. The crying is cathartic — Kit is processing trauma in real time.
- **Departure and Trust:** Eldeth leaving is the first voluntary party split. She goes not because she must, but because she believes she can do more good elsewhere. The party trusts her.

## Archivist Notes

### Patterns, Progress & Future Implications

- **Kit's Isolation Continues:** Kit was again the emotional outlier — her "gift" came through trauma (a nightmare fight she couldn't win), while everyone else received comfort, reunion, or mentorship. This isolation pattern from S10 continues and deepens.
- **Naos Rescue Arc:** A new major quest thread. Naos is in danger from the corrupted entity. Aeolus now has both personal motivation (love) and cosmic responsibility (save the realms).
- **The Corrupted Entity:** Named as female, from the Abyss, a twisted reflection of Psilofyr. She seeks to control death. Naos "dare not speak her name." This is almost certainly Zuggtmoy (Demon Queen of Fungi) from Out of the Abyss, though the campaign may use a homebrew name.
- **Growth Items:** DM confirmed all empowered trinkets are "growth items" that improve at level thresholds. Track upgrades going forward.
