## Session Analysis

### Narrative Summary

The party departed Velkynvelve after their long rest and began the 7-day journey to [[Sloobludop]] on the southern banks of [[04-World-Lore/Locations/The Darklake]]. Addison confirmed the full Velkynvelve armory inventory — the party is now well-equipped with chainmail, weapons, caltrops, 600 feet of silk rope, maps, rations, spell components, gold, and valuables, all loaded onto [[Roxanne]]'s chest and saddlebags.

The marching order established two abreast: [[Binks Stonevein]] and [[Kit Aluri]] scouting on foot at the front, [[Blarg]] walking behind them, [[Topsy]] and [[Turvy]] near Binks, and [[Darby Stonefeather]], [[Aeolus]], [[Amanita de'Champignon]], and [[Stool]] riding [[Roxanne]]. [[Derendil]] also traveling with the party.

Days 1–2 passed quietly. [[Blarg]] and [[Amanita de'Champignon]] began Underdark botany training — Amanita teaching Blarg about fungal equivalents of overworld plants. Amanita's teaching was enthusiastic but unfocused (Charisma 12, flat d20 of 6). Blarg's Insight of 9 meant no immediate skill bonus, but learning is underway. Blarg then rolled a Nature 23 looking for exotic specimens and discovered Ormu — a bioluminescent green moss that can be dried into phosphorescent powder with Faerie Fire-like applications. He harvested it with his sickle.

[[Kit Aluri]] spent the travel time maintaining and attuning to her new equipment — particularly [[Jorlan Duskryn]]'s cloak. She hummed quietly (Stealth 20 — nobody noticed) while oiling the garment. The humming created a magical resonance that was absorbed into the fabric, and the cloak revealed itself as a **Cloak of Elvenkind** — Kit is now attuned (Performance 16+Bless=18, Religion 7+Bless=10). During the attunement, Kit experienced a fleeting déjà vu — a memory at the tip of her mind that she recognized but couldn't place. Addison's connection froze mid-narration at this point (~00:35:35), and the content may have been lost.

Addison also clarified that Jorlan's bracers are **+1 AC (not Bracers of Defense)** — they work with armor but not with a shield.

[[Binks Stonevein]] held language classes teaching Undercommon and Sylvan. His Performance check was low (5+4=9), so students rolled Insight with disadvantage. [[Darby Stonefeather]] rolled a nat 20 (with disadvantage!) and excelled. [[Amanita de'Champignon]] rolled nat 1 and nat 20. [[Kit Aluri]] rolled a nat 1 on Insight and decided she was too busy with her weapons to attend. [[Aeolus]] specifically requested Sylvan lessons.

Day 3 was quiet. On Day 4, [[Amanita de'Champignon]] discovered a large fungal cavern (encounter roll of 4). She identified Timmask (explosive), Tongue of Madness (poisonous), Nightlight, and Waterorb. She searched for Nilhogg's Nose specifically but didn't find any (Investigation 6 even with Blarg's Help). Bless had worn off by this point. Amanita guided the party safely through the cavern.

[[Binks Stonevein]] and [[Aeolus]] discussed loading the Ring of Spell Storing (3 levels free, Darkness already stored). Options discussed: Shield of Faith, Invisibility, Bless, Disguise Self, Tasha's Hideous Laughter. No decision was made. The party also discussed Darkness tactics — agreeing they need a "safety word" meaning "get close to Aeolus, he's about to cast Darkness." The word was not chosen this session.

[[Topsy]] and [[Turvy]] were observed methodically rummaging through party gear daily — not malicious, described as thieves' habits. They're also working on fitting armor to themselves.

### Session Setting

Underdark tunnels between [[04-World-Lore/Locations/Velkynvelve]] and [[Sloobludop]]. Mostly monotonous tunnels with sporadic fungal growth and skittering sounds. Slight descent with increasing groundwater as the party approaches the Darklake region. Tunnels open and narrow in places; Roxy had to be coaxed through narrow sections. Fungal Cavern (Day 4) was a large chamber filled with fungi and mushrooms of all types.

### Themes & Emotional Beats

- **Domesticity after war:** The party's first true downtime since the Battle of Velkynvelve. After four sessions of escalating combat, this session was entirely about the mundane rhythms of travel — teaching, foraging, maintenance.
- **Kit's private rituals:** Kit's humming attunement scene was deeply personal. Her instinct to hide it (Stealth 20) reveals vulnerability she doesn't show the group. The déjà vu/memory is the first hint of something deeper in Kit's past connecting to the drow.
- **Blarg and Amanita's mentorship:** The botany partnership is growing organically. Blarg found Ormu on his own — teacher's not always needed when the student is ready.
- **Darby as surprising linguist:** A nat 20 on Undercommon Insight with disadvantage. Darby's learning moments tend to come in bursts of improbable excellence.
- **Aeolus's AC crisis:** "I'm a squishy warlock" with AC 12 is becoming a party concern. Multiple party members and the DM suggested solutions.
