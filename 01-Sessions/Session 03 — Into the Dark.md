## Session Analysis

### Narrative Summary

Session 03 opens in the immediate aftermath of the S02 escape. The party and four NPC companions land in the pool beneath [[Velkynvelve]], still under threat from drow above.

**The Pool:** The party struggles in deep water. [[Darby Stonefeather]] dives for supply chests (athletics nat 20+6). [[Binks Stonevein]] nearly drowns (athletics nat 1+3) and is rescued by [[Kit Aluri]] (athletics 17). [[Aeolus]] also struggles (athletics 3) and is later healed by Binks (Divine Spark, 2d8+4). [[Amanita de'Champignon]] spots a gray shape in the shallows (perception 25).

**Gray Ooze Combat:** A [[Gray Ooze]] emerges in the shallows and telepathically broadcasts "Flesh for the Faceless Lord" — the campaign's first reference to Juiblex. Amanita discovers her Halo of Spores ability for the first time (reaction, 2 necrotic). The ooze crits Amanita (nat 20, 14 total damage). [[Blarg]] heals Amanita with a 2nd-level Cure Wounds for 23 HP. [[Darby Stonefeather]] attacks with her whip (9 damage). Blarg's Guiding Bolt (13 radiant) drives the ooze to near-death; it flees.

**Travel and Route Choice:** Party runs north ~2 hours at fast pace. One exhaustion level applied. During short rest, the party debates routes: north toward [[Blingdenstone]] (~2 weeks), west toward Sloobludop (~10 days, off-path), or south toward [[Gracklstugh]] (duergar, unfriendly). [[Amanita de'Champignon]] senses [[Neverlight Grove]] to the south/west (nature 22 with advantage from [[Stool]]). Party chooses north.

**Mushroom Cavern and Dwarven Door:** Fire beetles and [[Timmask]] (devil's mushroom). Binks casts Pass Without Trace. Group stealth succeeds (Binks 30, Kit 30, Aeolus nat 20). [[Aeolus]]'s Devil's Sight (120ft, pierces magical darkness) discovered. Aeolus finds hidden dwarven door (nat 20 perception). [[Blarg]] reveals he doesn't speak Dwarvish. [[Eldeth]] speaks the safe word and opens the door. Session ends at threshold.
