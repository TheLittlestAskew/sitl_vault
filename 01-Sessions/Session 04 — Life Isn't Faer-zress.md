# Session 04 — Life Isn't Faer-zress
**Date:** 11/23/2025 | **Party Level:** 3

> ⚠ **No audio recording exists for this session.** Taylor was traveling home from PAX. All content sourced from Addison's (DM) written recap posted to Discord on 11/23/2025.

## Summary

Session 4 takes place entirely within the ancient Dwarvish/Duergar safe house discovered at the end of [[Session 03 — Into the Dark]]. The party rests with [[Topsy]] and [[Turvy]] taking first watch. Both twins undergo involuntary lycanthropic transformation and attack the party.

[[Binks Stonevein]] wakes first, raises defenses, and shouts a warning — calling for [[Kit Aluri]] not to kill them. Kit engages [[Topsy]], is bitten, and kills Topsy with her crowbar (hook behind neck, snapped sideways). [[Blarg]] casts Hold Person on [[Turvy]]; [[Aeolus]] hits with Eldritch Blast; [[Amanita de'Champignon]] fails Blindness through the narrow doorway; [[Darby Stonefeather]] stumbles through allies. Binks incapacitates Turvy with his Spiritual Weapon (giant glowing lute pick).

Session ends: Topsy dead, Turvy unconscious and restrained, party shaken by the revelation of hidden lycanthropy. The [[faerzress]] glow shifts ominously.

### Key Events

- **Topsy & Turvy transform involuntarily** during first watch — painful, uncontrolled lycanthropic transformation
- **Kit bitten by Topsy** — lycanthropy transmission status unknown (resolved S05: no infection)
- **Kit kills Topsy with crowbar** — hook catches behind neck, snapped sideways. Defining moment.
- **Binks KOs Turvy** non-lethally with Spiritual Weapon
- **Faerzress corruption** — the safe house's magical energy shifts after the violence

## Themes & Emotional Beats

- **Betrayal of Safety** — First shelter since captivity becomes a combat zone
- **Instinct vs. Mercy** — Binks calls for restraint; Kit hears and kills anyway
- **Hidden Curses** — Two companions concealing lycanthropy; trust fractures
- **The Crowbar as Instrument** — A tool becomes a weapon with weight
- **Corrupted Nature** — Faerzress responds to violence
