# Session 04 — [Title Pending]
**Date:** 12/07/2025 | **Party Level:** 3 | **Pursuit Level:** 3/5

## Summary

Session 4 opens in the immediate aftermath of the wererat attack from [[Session 03 — Into the Dark]]. The party has managed a short rest but not a long rest. [[Topsy]] is dead, killed by [[Kit Aluri]] with her crowbar during the fight. [[Turvy]] is unconscious, restrained, and tied to a chair at zero hit points. The [[faerzress]] energy suffusing the ancient dwarvish safe house has been observed behaving erratically.

### Key Events

**Crowbar Silvering:** Kit discovers her crowbar's tip has become silvered during or after killing Topsy. [[Amanita de'Champignon]] detects transmutation magic on it (Arcana 16). Kit confirms via History check (17, DC 15) it was not silvered before the fight. The source of the transmutation is unknown.

**The Turvy Debate:** Extended moral discussion about Turvy's fate. Kit wants him dead. [[Aeolus]] agrees. [[Darby Stonefeather]] takes a pacifist stance. Amanita wants to interrogate. [[Binks Stonevein]] provides critical context about [[Blingdenstone]]'s complicated history with wererats.

**DM Retroactive Correction:** Addison corrects Session 3 ruling — wererats normally DO have control over their transformations (homebrew). The attack was involuntary and abnormal.

**Turvy's Testimony:** Binks heals Turvy (Healing Word, 9 HP). Turvy wakes grief-stricken, claims involuntary transformation caused by corrupted faerzress. Reveals they used a substance ("jam") to control their lycanthropy, but it failed in this location. Describes seeing "dark tendrils" before losing control.

**Amanita's Madness:** Amanita casts Detect Magic near the corrupted faerzress. Senses chaotic mix of magical schools (transmutation, divination, illusion, abjuration). Fails Wisdom save (9), rolls d100 (81) — suffers short-term madness, madness level increased to 1. Stunned by hallucination of corrupted fungal lichen. Binks punches her out of it.

**Kit vs. Turvy:** Kit attempts to knock Turvy unconscious. Misses first swing (11), hits second (16, 8 damage to 1 HP). Delivers defining speech: "I killed your sister because she tried to kill me... I'm a fucking orc. I don't want to hear it." Fails unarmed strike (7). [[Blarg]] intervenes.

**Blarg's Compassion:** Blarg speaks privately with Turvy about shared loss of family, heals him (Cure Wounds, 8 HP). Turvy reveals orphaned wererats "used to take bets on who would go first."

**First Long Rest:** Party achieves first long rest since escaping [[Velkynvelve]]. Pursuit level stays at 3. All exhaustion cleared except Darby (1 level).

**Floor Collapse & Tunnels:** Party investigates a moved table. Amanita detects mycelial network beneath the floor (Investigation 19). She strikes the floor with a mithril-laced pickaxe that channels her magic — floor collapses. Three tunnel passages discovered: north (small, crawlable), west/down (stone ladder), deeper (slide).

**Binks' Augury:** Binks casts Augury ritual using [[Garl Glittergold]] talisman. Deeper path: "bad, but could be fun." Dwarven path: safe, boring. Kit rolls Insight (19) vs Binks' Deception (17) — confirms he's following his trickster god, not being manipulated.

**Cliffhanger:** Tunnels crumble. Binks fails Dex save, takes 7 damage, slides into the deeper tunnels. Session ends with the party split.
