# Session 06 — The Long Way Down

## Narrative Summary

Session 06 opens in the immediate aftermath of [[Binks Stonevein|Binks]]'s fall through the collapsing safe house floor at the end of [[Session 05 — Silvered|Session 05]]. Binks lands 20–25 feet below on a pile of bones and rubble, suffering 8 points of fall damage and losing both hearing and dark vision. The party organizes a rescue: [[Aeolus]] uses Spider Climb to descend the vertical shaft while the remaining party members rig spelunking gear with silk rope and pitons. [[Darby Stonefeather|Darby]] rolls a nat 20 on survival, setting up three secure lines in 10 minutes.

Once reunited, [[Blarg]] diagnoses Binks with a traumatic brain injury (medicine 25) and heals him with Cure Wounds (15 HP), restoring hearing and vision. [[Kit Aluri|Kit]] negotiates [[Turvy]]'s continued presence with the party; Turvy reveals he can control his lycanthropy and pledges not to bite allies. [[Eldeth]] remains at the safe house to watch over [[Topsy]]'s body.

Blarg activates Tremorsense (racial ability, 60 ft for 10 minutes) and detects two faint movement signatures. Turvy, in wererat form with Binks's Blessing of the Trickster for advantage on stealth, scouts ahead and is grabbed by a gray ooze clinging to the ceiling. **Combat 1** is swift: Blarg (Starry Wisp, 3 radiant), Aeolus (Eldritch Blast, 8 force), Binks (Guiding Bolt, 14 radiant — killing blow).

Beyond the ooze chamber, the party discovers [[Glabbagool]] — an awakened, sentient gelatinous cube. [[Amanita de'Champignon|Amanita]] befriends Glabbagool, trading a mushroom for 14 gold pieces. Darby retrieves a magical mace from within Glabbagool's body (STR 22, DEX 20 to avoid acid). Blarg's arcana check (22) suggests Glabbagool's sentience may be connected to the influx of chaotic fiendish energy affecting the Underdark. Amanita's nature check (24) reveals the iron grate above uses mithril-laced enchantments that filter inorganic matter while allowing organic matter to pass.

The party descends through increasingly flooded, acidic tunnels. Blarg wild shapes into a giant spider (twice this session), Binks rides his back, Aeolus takes 5 acid damage from a slip, and Amanita gets stuck in Blarg's web before Darby frees her. Darby attunes to the recovered mace via a high arcana roll (dirty 20), discovering it produces green flame, provides light, and deals +1 fire damage on hit.

An invisible Binks scouts ahead and ambush-kills a second gray ooze with a single Guiding Bolt from stealth (22 damage). The party navigates four passageways with weakened flagstone traps concealing deep pits (black ooze visible below); Amanita falls into one but is saved by Kit.

The party enters the [[Oozing Temple]] fountain room: seven wall niches, a broken clawed statue on a pedestal, and four "formless sculptures" that are actually four gray oozes. **Combat 3** involves the full party. Kit deals heavy slashing damage. Darby enters her **first rage** and wields the green flame mace to devastating effect, including a critical hit for 20 damage. Binks accidentally hits Darby with Guiding Bolt (reduced to 8 by Stone's Endurance). Blarg's Moonbeam and Amanita's Symbiotic Entity + Halo of Spores clean up the remaining oozes.

The defeated oozes' remains reform into a dragon statue whose eyes light up, revealing treasure in the fountain basin: 112 sp, 41 gp, 3 green gold bracelets (25 gp each), a +1 drow spider dagger, a Potion of Greater Healing, and Oil of Slipperiness. As the party collects loot, the wall cracks and water floods in rapidly. The session ends ankle-deep in rising water.

## Session Setting

Beneath the ancient Dwarvish/Duergar safe house first discovered in [[Session 03 — Into the Dark|Session 03]]. Day 2 post-[[04-World-Lore/Locations/Velkynvelve]] escape. The party descends through natural stone shafts, past a mithril-enchanted iron grate, through bone-littered caverns, [[Glabbagool]]'s corridor, and acidic flooded tunnels to reach the [[Oozing Temple]].
