# Session 06 — The Long Way Down

**Date:** December 21, 2025
**Party Level:** 3 (DM announced level 4 planning for future session)

---

## Narrative Summary

Session 06 opens in the immediate aftermath of [[Binks Stonevein|Binks]]'s fall through the collapsing safe house floor at the end of [[Session 05 — Silvered|Session 05]]. Binks lands 20–25 feet below on a pile of bones and rubble, suffering 8 points of fall damage and losing both hearing and dark vision. [[Aeolus]] uses Spider Climb to descend the shaft while the rest of the party rigs spelunking gear. [[Darby Stonefeather|Darby]] rolls a nat 20 on survival, setting up three secure lines in 10 minutes. [[Blarg]] slips during descent (3 bludgeoning), caught by [[Kit Aluri|Kit]] and Darby.

Blarg diagnoses Binks with a traumatic brain injury (medicine 25) and heals him with Cure Wounds (15 HP), restoring hearing and vision. Kit negotiates [[Turvy]]'s continued presence — Turvy reveals he can control his lycanthropy and pledges not to bite allies. Kit sets conditional terms: "The what's best for the party will come first in every situation." [[Eldeth]] remains at the safe house to watch over [[Topsy]]'s body.

Blarg activates Tremorsense (racial ability, 60 ft for 10 minutes) and detects two faint movement signatures. Turvy scouts in wererat form, is grabbed by a gray ooze on the ceiling. **Combat 1:** Blarg (Starry Wisp, 3 radiant), Aeolus (Eldritch Blast, 8 force), Binks (Guiding Bolt, 14 radiant — killing blow).

The party discovers [[Glabbagool]] — an awakened sentient gelatinous cube. [[Amanita de'Champignon|Amanita]] befriends it, trading a mushroom for 14 gold pieces. Darby retrieves a magical mace from within Glabbagool (STR 22, DEX 20). Blarg's arcana (22) suggests Glabbagool's sentience may be connected to the chaotic fiendish energy affecting the Underdark. Amanita's nature check (24) on the mithril grate reveals it filters inorganic matter while allowing organic matter through.

The party descends through acidic, flooded tunnels. Blarg wild shapes into a giant spider (twice), Binks rides his back. Aeolus takes 5 acid damage from a slip. Amanita gets stuck in Blarg's web, freed by Darby. Darby attunes to the mace (arcana dirty 20): green flame, +1 fire damage, provides light.

**Combat 2:** Invisible Binks scouts ahead, ambush-kills a gray ooze with a single Guiding Bolt from stealth (22 damage, instant kill).

The party navigates four passageways with weakened flagstone traps over deep pits (black ooze below). Amanita falls into one but is saved by Kit.

**Combat 3 (Oozing Temple):** Four gray oozes attack from wall niches. Kit (23 initiative, nat 20) slashes ooze A for 13 damage. Darby enters her first rage and crits ooze D for 20 damage. Binks's Guiding Bolt misses ooze C and hits Darby (8 after Stone's Endurance). Blarg casts Moonbeam on B+C. Amanita activates Symbiotic Entity, kills B (Shillelagh 12) and C (Halo of Spores 7 necrotic). Defeated oozes reform into a dragon statue — eyes light up, fountain opens revealing treasure.

**Loot:** 112 sp, 41 gp, 3 green gold bracelets (25 gp each), +1 drow spider dagger, Potion of Greater Healing, Oil of Slipperiness.

Wall cracks, water floods in rapidly. Session ends ankle-deep in rising water.
