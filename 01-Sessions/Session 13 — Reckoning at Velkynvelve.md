## Narrative Summary

Session 13 picks up at the top of the initiative round where S12 left off. The entire session is a single continuous combat encounter — the conclusion of the Battle of Velkynvelve that began in [[Session 12 — Return to Velkynvelve|Session 12]].

**Pre-session housekeeping** establishes several important updates: Addison confirms trinket empowerment stat bonuses (Kit +1 STR, Aeolus +1 WIS, Blarg +1 CHA, Darby +1 CHA and +1 INT). Taylor and Matt are given control of [[Topsy]] and [[Turvy]] respectively for combat. Both twins leveled up between sessions with Fey-Touched (Misty Step plus a 1st-level spell each). Turvy is assigned Silver Barbs; Topsy's spell is pending. Addison also confirms [[Stool]] has been empowered with Pacifying Spores, Dissonant Whispers, and Misty Step. Taylor reads the S12 POV journal aloud to the table — Addison praises it.

The combat resumes with [[Ilvara Mizzrym]] floating down to address the party in Undercommon, delivering a monologue about their foolishness in returning and offering surrender. She casts a mass healing spell affecting all surviving drow, partially undoing the party's progress. The battle becomes a grinding war of attrition with the party struggling against high-AC drow, poison damage, and Ilvara's devastating spellcasting.

Key combat developments: [[Blarg]] casts Healing Word on Kit (12 HP), uses Primal Savagery to break free of spider webbing, then later wild shapes into a bear. [[Darby Stonefeather]] is knocked unconscious by [[Shoor Vandree]]'s poisoned short sword crit (DC 13 Con save, failed by exactly 5 — poison-induced unconsciousness). [[Kit Aluri]] is dropped to 1 HP by the Drow Elite's crit (24 total damage — 12 piercing, 12 poison), triggering Relentless Endurance. Kit uses Riposte when the Elite misses on the first attack, dealing 14 damage. She struggles with attack rolls all session, missing repeatedly despite Action Surge.

[[Stool]] Misty Steps onto unconscious Darby's head and wakes her as an action — a moment of pure comedy amid the carnage. [[Binks Stonevein]] casts Tasha's Hideous Laughter on Ilvara ("Hey, Ilvara, why are you on the ground? Are you drowsy?") with disadvantage from Clementine's distraction — Ilvara rolls a 3 and 5, failing completely. She is prone and laughing, unable to act.

Ilvara casts Insect Plague (Concentration), a 20-foot swarm of spiders dealing 25 piercing damage (Con DC 15, half on save). The spell devastates everyone in range — including her own allies. [[Shoor Vandree]] fails the save and goes down, killed by Ilvara's own spell. The Drow Elite and Quaggoth also take heavy damage. [[Stool]] drops to 0 HP (1 death save success as of session end).

The turning point comes from unexpected quarters. [[Amanita de'Champignon]] and [[Aeolus]] spot [[Jorlan Duskryn]] on the north tower — wounded, leaning against the railing, eyes locked on Ilvara. He fires a crossbow bolt that strikes Ilvara in the neck. She grabs the bolt, looks up, and says: "From you, Jorlan? I didn't think you had the balls." Jorlan smiles. Simultaneously, [[Derendil]] leaps from a stalactite and crashes into Ilvara, knocking her off the spider and to the ground. A massive lift platform swings loose and crashes into [[Asha Vandree]] and a Quaggoth, dealing bludgeoning damage. Ilvara loses concentration on Insect Plague (Con save: rolled 5, failed). The swarm dissipates.

The quaggoths shift allegiance to [[Derendil]] as the new alpha, turning on the remaining drow. Turvy uses Silver Barbs to save Kit from the Drow Elite's attack, saying "Can't lose anymore" in Gnomish. [[Topsy]] puts a giant spider to sleep with a poisoned crossbow bolt (Con save failed). [[Blarg]] (bear form) claws and bites Ilvara for 20 total damage.

The killing blow belongs to [[Roxanne]]. Austin describes a "primal proxy rage moment" — Roxy charges Ilvara with a gore attack (27 damage: 4d8+5), impaling and trampling the High Priestess. Addison narrates: Ilvara screams in Undercommon "Unhand me, you brute!" before Roxy's horns tear through her. The momentum sends her flying — she lands in a bloody heap. Ilvara Mizzrym is dead. The primary antagonist arc from Session 1 is closed.

Session ends with Asha alive but critically wounded (<10 HP), one Drow Elite alive, and the quaggoths now allied with the party via Derendil. Sessions 13 and 14 are back-to-back (Sunday + Monday).

## Themes & Emotional Beats

- **Closure and Catharsis:** The arc that began in Session 1 ends here. Ilvara — the woman who enslaved, tortured, and hunted the party — dies not to a hero's blade but to a rothe's horns. The indignity is poetic.
- **Unlikely Heroes:** The killing blow goes to Roxy, a resurrected rothe. The turning point comes from Jorlan, a drow they barely knew. Derendil, a quaggoth they couldn't save in S02, saves them now. The party's compassion and loose ends pay off.
- **Frustration and Grit:** Kit's session is defined by missed attacks, near-death, and the mechanical frustration of a fighter who can't connect. Her resilience — Relentless Endurance, Second Wind twice, Riposte — keeps her standing through sheer stubbornness.
- **Found Family Under Fire:** Stool waking Darby. Turvy using Silver Barbs to save Kit ("Can't lose anymore"). Binks healing Kit twice. Blarg shielding Darby. The party protects each other at every cost.
- **Ilvara's Self-Destruction:** Her own Insect Plague kills Shoor. Her pride prevents surrender. Her contempt for Jorlan blinds her to the crossbow bolt. She is undone by her own cruelty.

### Encounter Summary

The Battle of Velkynvelve concludes with a decisive party victory after approximately 4-5 combat rounds in S13 (following ~3 rounds in S12). The encounter was a war of attrition with the party absorbing enormous damage — Kit triggered Relentless Endurance, Darby was knocked unconscious by poison, and Stool dropped to 0 HP. Blarg, Binks, and Aeolus provided critical healing and support to keep the front line standing.

The turning point was threefold: Jorlan's crossbow bolt (betrayal from within), Derendil's tackle (removing Ilvara from her elevated position), and Ilvara's own Insect Plague killing Shoor and damaging her allies. Binks's Tasha's Hideous Laughter locked Ilvara down for a full round, preventing any further spellcasting. Roxy's 27-damage gore attack finished what 12 sessions of pursuit began.

The primary antagonist arc — escape from Ilvara's prison, survive her pursuit, return to settle the score — is now complete. The party controls the field. Remaining threats: Asha (<10 HP, fled), one Drow Elite (wounded), and whatever lies beyond the outpost. Stool's critical condition (0 HP, 1 death save success) is the most urgent concern entering S14.
