## Narrative Summary

The party emerges from [[Psilofyr]]'s portal into the western passage of [[04-World-Lore/Locations/Velkynvelve]]. Rapport spores from [[Session 11 — Gifts_of_the_Carrion_King|Session 11]] remain active, granting telepathic communication. [[Binks Stonevein|Binks]] surveys the cavern (perception 23): significant battle damage, dead drow/quaggoth/spider corpses, a dead chasme in the webbing. [[Ilvara Mizzrym|Ilvara]] and [[Shoor Vandree|Shore]] inspect the boardwalk. [[Asha Vandree|Asha]] rides a burnt, injured giant spider. Garrison reduced by roughly half.

The party debates strategy and settles on rescuing [[Derendil]] and eliminating the drow threat. Binks casts Pass Without Trace. A patrol drow approaches. [[Blarg]] (by proxy) casts Hold Person — the drow fails (Wis 9 vs DC 15). Binks auto-crits with sneak attack: 15 damage kills the 13 HP drow instantly. Shore spots Binks and shouts: "You were a fool to return."

Kit holds the party in the tunnel, coordinating via telepathic spores. Ilvara casts Darkness on Binks. Kit charges through darkness with greatsword out, clips a drow (9 damage via Dex save), then kills Drow F with Precision Attack (max damage: double sixes, 16 total). Darby enters Axe Beak rage but can't find anyone in the dark. Stool finds Binks (perception 22) and provides Help. Amanita dodges Darby (nat 20 Dex), hits the Drow Elite with Poison Spray (6) and Halo of Spores (6 necrotic, doubled). Binks crits Spiritual Weapon on the Drow Elite (25 damage). [[Aeolus]] uses Devil's Sight to hit the Drow Elite with Eldritch Blast (7 damage).

Asha casts Guiding Bolt on Kit (15 radiant). Spiders web Binks (restrained). Shore casts a second Darkness on Kit. Kit perceives Drow D in darkness (perception 22 vs DC 15), kills him with greatsword + Trip Attack (18 total). Darby mounts Roxy mid-charge (Athletics dirty 20), spots Shore via Topsy's help, and hits him with the fire mace (16 damage). Aeolus breaks Shore's Darkness with Witch Bolt level 2 (22 lightning, Shore fails concentration).

Session ends mid-combat. Asha summons Spiritual Weapon (misses Kit). Shore and Drow Elite badly wounded. Ilvara, Asha, spiders, and quaggoths still active.
