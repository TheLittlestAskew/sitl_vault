## Narrative Summary

Session 9 is a short, exploration-and-discovery-focused session with no combat. The DM opens by retconning exhaustion levels — everyone is set to 1 level, and the 2024 exhaustion rules are clarified (-2 to all d20 tests, -5 to speed; NOT disadvantage as in 2014 rules). [[Kit Aluri]] and [[Darby Stonefeather]] are confirmed at Level 4 from their end-of-S07 level-up; other PCs remain at Level 3.

[[Glabbagool]], the sentient gelatinous cube, seeps up through the floor and rejoins the party. Unable to keep pace, the party compresses Glabbagool into [[Amanita de'Champignon]]'s waterskin (costing Amanita one day of water supply). The party scouts south through parallel tunnels. Kit centralizes herself in a near-meditative listening stance (Perception 19 adjusted). Amanita discovers a shallow pond with unusual fungal growth, the skeletal remains of a large Rothe with a magically sealed chest fused to its bones, and a sporadic flickering portal against the cave wall.

[[Aeolus]] identifies the chest as password-locked (Arcana 15 adjusted). Amanita channels magic through her mithril pickaxe (partial Wisdom check + Strength check) and pries it open. Inside: a Resurrection spell scroll (7th level) and a diamond component. The DM reveals Amanita's luck roll (flat d20 = 14) determined the scroll level. The party debates using the scroll on [[Topsy]] (whose body is sealed in the rebuilt safe house and inaccessible) versus the Rothe. Kit explicitly favors Topsy if given a real choice but the body is unreachable.

[[Binks Stonevein]] casts the 7th-level Resurrection from the scroll (DC 17 spellcasting ability check; with Guidance, Blarg's Help for advantage, he rolls 21 adjusted). The Rothe returns to life — larger than normal, fully fleshed with fur and anatomy, chest permanently fused to its back. [[Darby Stonefeather]] gets pinned against the wall for 6 bludgeoning damage. Aeolus casts Speak with Animals but the Rothe has no memories. [[Eldeth]] reads a Dwarvish collar tag: "Roxanne." The chest's password is also "Roxanne" and it functions as extradimensional storage.

[[Blarg]] investigates the portal (Arcana 23 adjusted): neutral origin, connected to the fungal network, destabilized by corrupted Faerzress. Could be temporarily stabilized but destination and timing are uncertain. A deep, earthy rumbling sound emanates from beyond the portal. The session ends on a cliffhanger with the party undecided.
