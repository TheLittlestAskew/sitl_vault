## Session Analysis

### Narrative Summary

Session 14 opens in the immediate aftermath of the Battle of Velkynvelve (S12-S13). The party stands battered but victorious on the cavern floor with [[Ilvara Mizzrym]] dead, [[Shoor Vandree]] dead, and the surviving drow in shock. Kit has 3 HP. Blarg's bear form is coughing blood. The session pivots from combat to aftermath, diplomacy, grief, blasphemy, and travel planning.

[[Binks Stonevein]] taunts the drow elite while [[Asha Vandree]] attempts to cast Darkness — stopped by Binks's Command (Halt). [[Darby Stonefeather]] attempts to intimidate Asha with an awkward threat and rolls a 5 with disadvantage. A voice from above — [[Artaxle]], a young drow — calls down that [[Jorlan Duskryn]] is dying. Kit rallies despite her injuries and delivers an impassioned speech asking the drow to let her pay respects. She rolls a nat 20 on Persuasion (with Binks Help). The drow elite surrenders, saying Kit has earned his "respect" in halting Common.

Asha casts Darkness and flees. Darby kills her with an attack of opportunity (Fire Mace, 20 damage — spike embedded in skull). [[Garruk]] escapes with Ilvara's body and Shoor's riding lizard.

Kit reaches Jorlan in the northern watchpost. In a deeply emotional exchange, Jorlan passes on lore about [[Drizzt Do'Urden]], the [[Society of Brilliance]] (including mind flayer [[Grazilaxx]]), and faith in [[Artaxle]]. Jorlan grips Kit's hand and dies. Kit closes his eyes and takes his gear.

Binks enters the Shrine of Lolth and casts Ceremony (Atonement). Nat 1 Insight (lowered DC), Religion 15. He takes 7 psychic damage and 1 madness point (stunned), but the Ring of Mind Shielding blocks Lolth. Half the statue transforms into [[Garl Glittergold]] giving Lolth the middle finger. Faerzress shifts purple to gold. Entire party Blessed 24 hours.

Party decides on [[Sloobludop]] as next destination (8 days travel). Artaxle declines to join, heading to [[Menzoberranzan]] with false intel. Long rest taken at Velkynvelve.
