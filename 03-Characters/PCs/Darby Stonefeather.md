## Description / Appearance

- Enormous Goliath — described as visually imposing
- Age: 20
- Movement speed: 35ft (S02)

## Backstory

- Imprisoned at [[03-World-Lore/Locations/Velkynvelve]] for 5 days as of S01
- Socially inexperienced — a sheltered farmer who has never left her family's farm (the Stonefeather Roost, a multigenerational axebeak farm)
- Overwhelmed by the diversity of people and creatures around her; everything is a first
- S01 marks her first wave to another person, first telepathic conversation (via Stool's rapport spores), and first failed social deception

## Personality

- Shy, socially awkward, but physically fearless
- Easily overwhelmed by new experiences ("This is too much." — S01)
- Developing rapidly under pressure; learning faster than she lets on
- Tender and sentimental — held her father's totem close upon finding it (S02)
- Despite shyness, capable of explosive physical action (shoving Asha Vandree off a cliff without hesitation)

## Abilities & Class Features

- **Goliath Strength:** Advantage on strength checks. Broke her own shackles with a massive strength roll (S02). Cranked the platform lift solo, outperforming quaggoths (S01, strength 19).
- **Powerful Build:** Can carry, push, drag, and lift more than typical
- **Rage of the Wilds:** Axebeak form (+4 beak slashing, 50ft speed)
- **Sentinel feat:** Guardian + Halt
- **Movement speed:** 35ft
- Alignment: Lawful Good
- Faith: Dollyism
