## Description / Appearance

- Enormous Goliath — described as visually imposing
- Age: 20
- Movement speed: 35ft (S02)
- No darkvision — relies on party or light sources in the Underdark
- As of S06: carries a green flame mace that provides bright light when lit

## Backstory

- Imprisoned at [[04-World-Lore/Locations/Velkynvelve]] for 5 days as of S01
- Socially inexperienced — a sheltered farmer who has never left her family's farm (the Stonefeather Roost, a multigenerational axebeak farm)
- Faith: Dollyism

## Personality

- Shy, socially awkward, but physically fearless
- Easily overwhelmed by new experiences ("This is too much." — S01)
- Developing rapidly under pressure; learning faster than she lets on
- Tender and sentimental — held her father's totem close upon finding it (S02)
- Despite shyness, capable of explosive physical action (shoving Asha Vandree off a cliff without hesitation)
- Pacifist stance on killing helpless creatures — opposed killing Turvy (S05)
- S06: beginning to "lock in" — first rage, first magical weapon, first time providing light to the party
- S08: Ancestral awakening — first rage with spectral manifestation. The farm girl is carrying ancient power.
- S09: Farmer instincts re-emerge — immediately claimed animal handler role for [[Roxanne]].

## Abilities & Class Features

- **Goliath Strength:** Advantage on strength checks. Broke her own shackles (S02). Cranked platform lift solo (S01, strength 19).
- **Powerful Build:** Can carry, push, drag, and lift more than typical
- **Stone's Endurance:** Reaction to reduce damage. Used to reduce Binks's friendly fire from 9 to 8 (S06).
- **Rage:** First entered rage in S06 Combat 3. S08: First rage with spectral manifestation — ax beak feathers, whirlwind, radiant energy.
- **Rage of the Wilds:** Three rage forms available. Fourth option added S11:
  - Axebeak form (+4 beak slashing, 50ft speed) — base subclass
  - **Axe Beak (Empowered Totem, S11):** Movement increases to 50ft; all attacks add +D4 slashing damage on hit.
- **Sentinel feat:** Guardian + Halt
- **Movement speed:** 35ft
- Alignment: Lawful Good
