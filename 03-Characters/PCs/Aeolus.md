## Abilities & Class Features

- **Devil's Sight:** 120ft darkvision that sees through both magical and non-magical darkness. Discovered S03. DM explicitly noted this as a game-changing tactical asset for scouting. Distinct from standard 60ft darkvision — this is an invocation, not a racial trait. First used to spot hidden dwarven door and identify two escape passages.
- **Eldritch Blast:** Ranged spell attack. Used S01 (7 force damage vs spider) and S02 (missed quaggoth). DM noted ranged spell attacks should have disadvantage within 5ft — waived for S01, enforced from S02.
- **Find Familiar:** Cast in S01 shrine using incense as material components. Summoned a bat. Bat retrieved gemstone from spider statue eye before being destroyed.
- **Shocking Grasp:** Melee spell attack. Used S02 (14, just hit spider's AC of 14; 4 lightning damage). Prevents opportunity attacks — used to disengage safely.
- **Purify Food and Water:** Available; can mitigate poisonous food found in the Underdark (noted S03).
- **Fey Touched (Intelligence)**
- **Skilled feat**
