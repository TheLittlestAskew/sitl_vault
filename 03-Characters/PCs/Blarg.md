## Description / Appearance

- Dwarf — no detailed physical description given in S01–S02 beyond standard dwarf build
- Cheerful demeanor even in prison; humming tunes in the corner of the cell (S01)

## Backstory

- Arrived at [[03-World-Lore/Locations/Velkynvelve]] 2 days before S01
- Fell through a sinkhole in the [[Evermoors]] (a surface region) and was captured by drow below (S01)
- Arrived with a drow prisoner exchange (S01)

## Personality

- Cheerful, content, philosophically detached — treats imprisonment like a minor inconvenience
- Genuinely delighted by identifying Zurkhwood as edible mushroom wood (nature 26, S01)
- Unfazed by danger; approaches chaos with curiosity
- Practical when needed: immediately followed Amanita to the elite barracks because "she seems like she knows what she's doing" (S02)
- Self-aware humor about his own combat mishaps

## Abilities & Class Features

- **Wild Shape (Circle of the Moon):** First used S02 — wolf form. Bite attack (+4 to hit, reach 5ft). Pack Tactics (advantage when ally within 5ft — forgotten during S02, noted for future). 2024 rules allow speech in beast form (table discovery S02).
- **Poison Spray:** Used S01 vs giant spider. 20 to hit, 1 damage (spider appeared immune to poison damage); second use 20 to hit (14+mod), 3 damage.
- **Produce Flame:** Used S02 vs quaggoth (1 fire damage).
- **Nature Check:** Nat 26 (S01) identified platform material as Zurkhwood.
- **Perception:** 23 in S01 shrine — spotted all features: 8 gemstone eyes, trapdoor, altar with candlesticks.
