# Prince Derendil

Quaggoth prisoner at [[03-World-Lore/Locations/Velkynvelve]] who claims to be a polymorphed elven prince.

## Description / Appearance

- Quaggoth body — large, ape-like Underdark creature
- Mannerisms are "very much not quaggoth-like" (S02) — does not exhibit typical quaggoth viciousness

## Personality

- Speaks a language Kit could not identify (INT 5 check, S02)
- Non-hostile despite quaggoth form
- Brave — leapt over [[Jorlan Duskryn]] to help [[Topsy]] and [[Turvy]] during the escape

## Key Events

- **[[Session 01 — Prisoners of the Underdark]]:** Observed by Kit and Darby as one of the non-hostile quaggoths in the prison area.
- **[[Session 02 — Come Get Your Shit]]:** During escape, leapt over Jorlan along the wall, past a quaggoth, and landed behind Topsy and Turvy to help them. Was caught by other quaggoths rushing from their den. Party could not see what happened to him as they jumped.
