# Ront

Orc prisoner at [[04-World-Lore/Locations/Velkynvelve]]. Second sacrifice.

## Personality

- Bully who shoved other prisoners around until Kit shoved back (S01)
- Kit and Ront came to "something like an understanding — not friendship, something more practical. He watched my back. I watched his." (S01, Kit's POV)
- Lost his temper at the wrong moment

## Key Events

- **[[Session 01 — Prisoners of the Underdark]]:** Attempted to rush a guard on the bridge, knocked [[Shuushar]] over the edge into the pool. Was the second sacrifice to the giant spiders. Kit spotted his body wrapped in webbing high in the stalactites with a dirty 20 investigation check (120ft darkvision).
