Not described — referenced only by [[Blarg]] and [[Chris]] in S11.

## Backstory

Carbra is Blarg's adoptive goblin shaman. Raised Blarg despite him being a dwarf. Known to [[Chris]] (fae goblin leader) from inter-tribal gatherings. Blarg does not speak Dwarvish because he was raised by Carbra's goblin tribe, not by dwarves.

## Key Events

- **[[Session 11 — Gifts_of_the_Carrion_King|Session 11]]:** Referenced by Blarg when speaking to Chris. Chris recognized Carbra's name and confirmed inter-tribal connections. First time Carbra has been named in the campaign.

## Relationships

- **[[Blarg]]:** Adoptive parent. Raised Blarg in a goblin tribe. Source of Blarg's goblin cultural identity.
- **[[Chris]]:** Knew Carbra from inter-tribal gatherings.
