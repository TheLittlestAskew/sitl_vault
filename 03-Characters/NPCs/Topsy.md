# Topsy

Deep gnome prisoner at [[03-World-Lore/Locations/Velkynvelve]]. Twin sibling of [[Turvy]]. Killed by [[Kit Aluri]] during an involuntary lycanthropic transformation in [[Session 04 — Life Isn't Faer-zress]].

## Description / Appearance

- Small deep gnome; squirmy, quick with hands
- In wererat hybrid form during transformation (S04)

## Personality

- Chatty, "maybe a little annoying" (S02 DM description)
- Plays pranks on drow guards (S01)
- Kept [[Aeolus]]'s spirits up during captivity (S01)

## Backstory

- Natural-born wererat (not infected); hid lycanthropy from the party
- Used a substance called "jam" to maintain control of transformations
- Orphan along with [[Turvy]]; "We used to take bets on who would go first"
- From the [[Blingdenstone]] area; reluctant to return there

## Key Events

- **[[Session 01 — Prisoners of the Underdark]]:** Introduced as one of two deep gnome twins. Kept Aeolus company. Played pranks on guards.
- **[[Session 02 — Come Get Your Shit]]:** Off shift when chaos erupted. Attacked a drow with [[Turvy]] during escape. Nearly knocked off the rope bridge (barely passed DEX save after drow nat 20 attack). [[Derendil]] leapt to help. Escaped into pool.
- **[[Session 04 — Life Isn't Faer-zress]]:** Involuntarily transformed into wererat during first watch due to corrupted faerzress. Attacked party. Bit [[Kit Aluri]]. Kit killed Topsy with her crowbar — hook caught behind neck, snapped sideways. Body covered with blanket; no burial performed.

## Cause of Death

Killed by [[Kit Aluri]] with silvered crowbar during involuntary lycanthropic attack. Crowbar hook caught behind Topsy's neck; Kit snapped it sideways, breaking her neck. ([[Session 04 — Life Isn't Faer-zress]])

## Notes

- Taylor and Matt rolled for Topsy and Turvy during combat
- Wererat nature was not known by party until the S04 transformation
- No burial or death ritual performed; body covered with blanket. Blarg asked Turvy about Svirfneblin death customs but no resolution before floor collapsed (S05)
