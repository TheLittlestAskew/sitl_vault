# Topsy

Deep gnome prisoner at [[03-World-Lore/Locations/Velkynvelve]]. Twin sibling of [[Turvy]].

## Personality

- Squirmy, quick with hands, chatty, "maybe a little annoying" (S02 DM description)
- Plays pranks on drow guards (S01)
- Kept [[Aeolus]]'s spirits up during captivity (S01)

## Key Events

- **[[Session 01 — Prisoners of the Underdark]]:** Introduced as one of two deep gnome twins. Kept Aeolus company.
- **[[Session 02 — Come Get Your Shit]]:** Off shift when chaos erupted. Attacked a drow with [[Turvy]] during escape. Nearly knocked off the rope bridge (barely passed DEX save after drow nat 20 attack). [[Derendil]] leapt to help. Topsy escaped into pool.

## Notes

- Taylor and Matt roll for Topsy and Turvy during combat
- Wererat nature (revealed later in campaign; not known by party in S01–S02)
- Reluctant to return to [[03-World-Lore/Locations/Blingdenstone]] — reason unknown
