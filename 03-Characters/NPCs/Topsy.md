# Topsy

Deep gnome prisoner at [[04-World-Lore/Locations/Velkynvelve]]. Twin sibling of [[Turvy]]. Killed by [[Kit Aluri]] during an involuntary lycanthropic transformation in [[Session 04 — Life Isn't Faer-zress]]. **Resurrected by the Carrion King ([[Psilofyr]]) in [[SITL_11_030126_Gifts_of_the_Carrion_King|Session 11]]** via mycelial fibers. Currently alive but weak.

## Description / Appearance

- Small deep gnome; squirmy, quick with hands
- In wererat hybrid form during transformation (S04)
- Post-resurrection (S11): wrapped in mycelial fibers, pale, weak but breathing

## Personality

- Chatty, "maybe a little annoying" (S02 DM description)
- Plays pranks on drow guards (S01)
- Kept [[Aeolus]]'s spirits up during captivity (S01)
- Post-resurrection: weak but characteristically upbeat — "I feel like absolute garbage, but you look worse than I did. Are you okay?" (to Kit, S11)

## Backstory

- Natural-born wererat (not infected); hid lycanthropy from the party
- Used a substance called "jam" to maintain control of transformations
- Orphan along with [[Turvy]]; "We used to take bets on who would go first"
- From the [[04-World-Lore/Locations/Blingdenstone]] area; reluctant to return there

## Death and Resurrection

**Cause of Death:** Killed by [[Kit Aluri]] with silvered crowbar during involuntary lycanthropic attack. Crowbar hook caught behind Topsy's neck; Kit snapped it sideways, breaking her neck. ([[Session 04 — Life Isn't Faer-zress]])

**Body Status (S04-S10):** Covered with blanket in the ancient Dwarvish safe house. No burial performed. When the safe house's self-repairing magic sealed the room, Topsy's body was entombed inside and became inaccessible. Kit advocated for resurrection in S09 but the body could not be reached.

**Resurrection (S11):** The Carrion King ([[Psilofyr]]) retrieved Topsy's body from the sealed safe house and resurrected her using mycelial fibers. Topsy was found alive but weak in a zurkhwood tent, wrapped in mycelial fibers. [[Binks Stonevein]] made a healing salve (medicine nat 24) to strengthen her recovery.

## Key Events

- **[[Session 01 — Prisoners of the Underdark]]:** Introduced as one of two deep gnome twins. Kept Aeolus company. Played pranks on guards.
- **[[Session 02 — Come Get Your Shit]]:** Off shift when chaos erupted. Attacked a drow with [[Turvy]] during escape. Nearly knocked off the rope bridge (barely passed DEX save after drow nat 20 attack). [[Derendil]] leapt to help. Escaped into pool.
- **[[Session 04 — Life Isn't Faer-zress]]:** Involuntarily transformed into wererat during first watch due to corrupted faerzress. Attacked party. Bit [[Kit Aluri]]. Kit killed Topsy with her crowbar. Body covered with blanket.
- **[[SITL_11_030126_Gifts_of_the_Carrion_King|Session 11]]:** Resurrected by the Carrion King. Found in zurkhwood tent wrapped in mycelial fibers. Binks made healing salve (medicine 24). Topsy spoke to Kit — "Oh, hey, hey, Kit. What? I feel like absolute garbage, but you look worse than I did." Kit fell to her knees sobbing upon seeing Topsy alive. Traveling with party through Velkynvelve portal.
