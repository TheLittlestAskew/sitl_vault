# Roxy

> **Type:** NPC Companion
> **Race:** Rothe (Skeletal)
> **Status:** Active — animated skeleton
> **Owner / Controller:** [[Amanita de'Champignon]]

---

## Description / Appearance

A skeletal rothe (Underdark cattle) reanimated by [[Amanita de'Champignon]] using Speak with Dead / wild magic. Bones only — no flesh, no hide. Cannot support weight on zurkhwood bridges due to skeletal structure.

---

## Backstory

Found as a rothe skeleton in the mushroom caverns below the upper cavern (S08). Amanita used her magic to resurrect it. The animation is controlled by a magic word.

---

## Abilities

- **Magic Word Activation:** Saying "Roxanne" (the full name) animates Roxy. Amanita insists on the full name.
- **Limitations:** Cannot support weight on zurkhwood bridges. Skeletal; no flesh or muscle.
- **Duration:** Unknown — open question whether the animation is permanent or temporary.

---

## Relationships

- **[[Amanita de'Champignon]]:** Creator and controller. Amanita named her and treats her as a companion/pet.

---

## Key Events

- **[[Session 08 - The Whirlwind and the Depths|S08]]:** Resurrected by Amanita in the mushroom caverns. Named "Roxanne" — "You have to say the whole thing."
