# Shoor Vandree

Drow warrior and current lieutenant/lover of [[Ilvara Mizzrym]] at [[04-World-Lore/Locations/Velkynvelve]].

## Backstory

- Replaced [[Jorlan Duskryn]] as Ilvara's favored lieutenant and lover (Kit's nat 20 history, S01)
- Younger captain (S01)

## Key Events

- **[[Session 01 — Prisoners of the Underdark]]:** Mentioned by guards. Not directly interacted with. Kit identified his role via history check.
- **[[Session 02 — Come Get Your Shit]]:** DM mentions his quarters as a possible scavenging location but Kit does not go there. Not directly encountered during escape.
- **[[Session 12 — Return to Velkynvelve|Session 12]]:** Spotted [[Binks Stonevein|Binks]] after the patrol drow kill and shouted: "You were a fool to return." Cast Darkness on Kit's position (second Darkness sphere, overlapping Ilvara's). Hit by [[Darby Stonefeather|Darby]]'s fire mace for 16 damage (24 to hit). Held Darkness concentration after Darby's hit. **Darkness concentration broken by [[Aeolus]]'s Witch Bolt (level 2): 22 lightning damage, failed concentration save.** Badly wounded at session end. Still active.

## Key Quotes

- "You were a fool to return." — S12 [Serious/Important]

## Relationships

- **[[Ilvara Mizzrym]]:** Current lover and lieutenant
- **[[Jorlan Duskryn]]:** Replaced Jorlan; the source of Jorlan's grudge
