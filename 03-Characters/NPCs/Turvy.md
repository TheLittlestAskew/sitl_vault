# Turvy

## Description / Appearance
Deep gnome. Young by gnomish standards. In wererat hybrid form, was described as "changing slower" before reverting. Small enough that a hand sticks out from under a blanket noticeably. In wererat form, described as a dog-sized rat that can climb with ease.

## Backstory
- Born with lycanthropy (natural-born wererat, not infected)
- Orphan — "Orphans are not. We used to take bets on who would go first? Because we just knew. Supposed to be me." ([[Session 05 — Silvered]])
- From the [[04-World-Lore/Locations/Blingdenstone]] area
- Captured by the drow and imprisoned at [[04-World-Lore/Locations/Velkynvelve]]

## Personality
- Grief-stricken but resolving after Topsy's death
- Speaks Gnomish (standard dialect; Topsy spoke Gnomish Creole). Also speaks Common (DM retcon, S06).
- Growing more purposeful — volunteered to help rescue Binks, pledged to control lycanthropy
- S11: Overjoyed at Topsy's resurrection. Speaking Gnomish to her at the zurkhwood tent.

## Abilities & Class Features
- **Lycanthropy (Wererat):** Natural-born. DM homebrew: wererats normally have more control than werewolves. Still affected by the moon. "Jam" substance used for control; failed in faerzress-corrupted safe house (S04).
- **Wererat Scouting:** In rat form, navigates narrow tunnels. Advantage on stealth with Blessing of the Trickster.
- **Wererat Combat:** Slashing attacks in hybrid form.

## Relationships
- **[[Topsy]]:** Sister — killed in S04, resurrected in S11. Overjoyed at her return. Speaking Gnomish to her at the tent.
- **[[Kit Aluri]]:** Evolved from hostile to conditional alliance (S06). Kit killed Topsy and openly wanted to kill Turvy. Terms set: party first.
- **[[Blarg]]:** Compassionate. Shared own family loss, gave moon flask (S05).
- **[[Binks Stonevein]]:** Pragmatic ally. Granted Blessing of the Trickster for scouting (S06). Binks made healing salve for Topsy (S11).
- **[[Eldeth]]:** Offered to watch over Topsy's body (S06). Departed (S11).
