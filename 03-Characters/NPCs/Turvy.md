# Turvy

## Description / Appearance
Deep gnome. Young by gnomish standards (younger than Binks). In wererat hybrid form, was described as "changing slower" before reverting. Small enough that a hand sticks out from under a blanket noticeably. In wererat form, described as a dog-sized rat that can climb with ease.

## Backstory
- Born with lycanthropy (natural-born wererat, not infected)
- Orphan — "Orphans are not. We used to take bets on who would go first? Because we just knew. Supposed to be me." ([[Session 05 — Silvered]])
- From the [[Blingdenstone]] area (implied by connection to the svirfneblin wererat community)
- Was captured by the drow and imprisoned at [[Velkynvelve]] along with the party

## Personality
- Grief-stricken but resolving after Topsy's death
- Speaks Gnomish (standard dialect; Topsy spoke Gnomish Creole). Also speaks Common (DM retcon, S06).
- Expressed fatalism about their orphan status and expected death
- Growing more purposeful — volunteered to help rescue Binks, pledged to control lycanthropy

## Abilities & Class Features
- **Lycanthropy (Wererat):** Natural-born wererat. DM ruling (homebrew): wererats normally have control over their transformations, more so than werewolves. However, still affected by the moon (DM homebrew). Topsy and Turvy's transformation in S04 was involuntary and abnormal, caused by corrupted faerzress.
- Used a substance called "jam" to maintain control of lycanthropy — failed in the faerzress-corrupted safe house
- **Wererat Scouting:** In rat form, can navigate narrow tunnels with ease. Has advantage on stealth when granted Blessing of the Trickster by Binks.
- **Wererat Combat:** Can make slashing attacks in hybrid form (missed in S06 Combat 1).

## Relationships
- **[[Topsy]]:** Sister (deceased). Turvy's grief at her death is the defining emotional moment of S04-S05. Arranged her body on the bed at the safe house (S06). Eldeth offered to watch over her.
- **[[Kit Aluri]]:** Evolved from hostile to conditional alliance (S06). Kit killed Topsy and openly wanted to kill Turvy. In S06, Kit asked Turvy what he wanted to do, set clear terms: "The what's best for the party will come first in every situation." Turvy accepted. Kit internally acknowledged Turvy's wererat form is "really convenient sometimes."
- **[[Blarg]]:** Compassionate. Blarg shared his own experience of family loss, encouraged Turvy to find new purpose, healed him, gave him the moon flask. Discussed Topsy's burial rites with him (S06 retcon).
- **[[Binks Stonevein]]:** Pragmatic. Binks provided historical context about Blingdenstone wererats. Granted Turvy Blessing of the Trickster for stealth scouting (S06).
- **[[Amanita de'Champignon]]:** Curious/sympathetic.
- **[[Darby Stonefeather]]:** Pacifist. Darby opposed killing Turvy but would not block the group.
- **[[Eldeth]]:** Eldeth offered to watch over Topsy's body at the safe house while Turvy descended with the party (S06).

## Key Events

### [[Session 01 — Prisoners of the Underdark]]
- Introduced as one of two deep gnome twins. Turvy usually only mutters in a strange gnomish dialect; Topsy translates.

### [[Session 02 — Come Get Your Shit]]
- Attacked a drow with Topsy during escape. Stool latched onto his back. Escaped into pool.

### [[Session 04 — Life Isn't Faer-zress]]
- Involuntarily transformed into wererat during first watch with Topsy
- Incapacitated by Binks's Spiritual Weapon (non-lethal KO)
- Tied to a chair, unconscious at 0 HP

### [[Session 05 — Silvered]]
- Binks healed him (Healing Word, 9 HP) and woke him
- Grief-stricken upon learning Topsy is dead
- Claimed the faerzress corruption caused involuntary transformation
- Received moon flask from Blarg
- Left in the safe house when the floor collapsed

### [[Session 06 — The Long Way Down]]
- Arranged Topsy's body on the bed at the safe house. Discussed burial with Blarg — said "This place doesn't feel right" for burial.
- When Kit asked what he would do, said: "B needs help. Gnomes protect gnomes. At least that's what they should. Top would want that."
- Pledged to Kit that he can control his lycanthropy: "I think I got a handle on this... It is a curse, but it's one I can use. Won't use it if you tell me no."
- Kit set conditional terms. Turvy accepted without resistance.
- Shifted to wererat form and descended the shaft with ease.
- Sent to scout ahead with Binks's Blessing of the Trickster (advantage on stealth). Grabbed by gray ooze on ceiling. Escaped grapple but fell. Freed by party in Combat 1.
- Sent back to safe house to inform Eldeth the party was pressing on. Returned to party (timing unclear).
