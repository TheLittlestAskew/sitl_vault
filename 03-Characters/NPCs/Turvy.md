# Turvy

## Description / Appearance
Deep gnome. Young by gnomish standards (younger than Binks). In wererat hybrid form, was described as "changing slower" before reverting. Small enough that a hand sticks out from under a blanket noticeably.

## Backstory
- Born with lycanthropy (natural-born wererat, not infected)
- Orphan — "Orphans are not. We used to take bets on who would go first? Because we just knew. Supposed to be me." ([[Session 05 — Silvered]])
- From the [[Blingdenstone]] area (implied by connection to the svirfneblin wererat community)
- Was captured by the drow and imprisoned at [[Velkynvelve]] along with the party

## Personality
- Grief-stricken and emotionally raw after Topsy's death
- Speaks Gnomish (standard dialect; Topsy spoke Gnomish Creole)
- Expressed fatalism about their orphan status and expected death
- Despite grief, attempted to communicate and explain what happened

## Abilities & Class Features
- **Lycanthropy (Wererat):** Natural-born wererat. DM ruling (homebrew): wererats normally have control over their transformations, more so than werewolves. However, still affected by the moon (DM homebrew). Topsy and Turvy's transformation in S04 was involuntary and abnormal, caused by corrupted faerzress.
- Used a substance called "jam" to maintain control of lycanthropy — failed in the faerzress-corrupted safe house

## Inventory / Loot

| Item | Source | Status |
|---|---|---|
| Moon-Linked Gemstone Flask | Given by [[Blarg]] (S05) | Carried — lunar cycle enchantment to help control lycanthropy |

## Relationships
- **[[Topsy]]:** Sister (deceased). Turvy's grief at her death is the defining emotional moment of S04-S05. "It wasn't supposed to be you. It was supposed to be me."
- **[[Kit Aluri]]:** Hostile/tense. Kit killed Topsy and openly wanted to kill Turvy. Kit delivered the "I'm a fucking orc" speech directly to his face. Kit attempted to knock him out multiple times.
- **[[Blarg]]:** Compassionate. Blarg shared his own experience of family loss, encouraged Turvy to find new purpose, healed him (Cure Wounds, 8 HP), and gave him the moon flask.
- **[[Binks Stonevein]]:** Pragmatic. Binks provided historical context about Blingdenstone wererats. Healed Turvy with Healing Word (9 HP) to wake him for interrogation.
- **[[Amanita de'Champignon]]:** Curious/sympathetic. Amanita patted his head; [[Stool]] gave him a slimy hug. Amanita wanted to interrogate rather than kill.
- **[[Darby Stonefeather]]:** Pacifist. Darby opposed killing Turvy but would not block the group.
- **Drow:** If left alone, would likely be captured, tortured, and killed by the pursuing drow.

## Key Events

### [[Session 01 — Prisoners of the Underdark]]
- Introduced as one of two deep gnome twins. Turvy usually only mutters in a strange gnomish dialect; Topsy translates.

### [[Session 02 — Come Get Your Shit]]
- Attacked a drow with Topsy during escape. Stool latched onto his back. Escaped into pool.

### [[Session 04 — Life Isn't Faer-zress]]
- Involuntarily transformed into wererat during first watch with Topsy
- Incapacitated by Binks's Spiritual Weapon (non-lethal KO)
- Tied to a chair, unconscious at 0 HP

### [[Session 05 — Silvered]]
- Binks healed him (Healing Word, 9 HP) and woke him
- Grief-stricken upon learning Topsy is dead — saw her arm sticking out from under blanket
- Claimed the faerzress corruption caused involuntary transformation — described the light changing and blacking out
- Revealed use of "jam" to control lycanthropy that failed in the safe house
- Kit attempted to knock him out (hit 16, 8 dmg to 1 HP; then missed unarmed strike)
- Blarg intervened, spoke with him privately, healed him (Cure Wounds, 8 HP to 9 HP total)
- Received moon flask from Blarg
- Cried himself to sleep
- Left in the safe house when the floor collapsed — fate unresolved; separated from party
