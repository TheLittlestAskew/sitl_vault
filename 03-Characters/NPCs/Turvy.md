# Turvy

Deep gnome prisoner at [[03-World-Lore/Locations/Velkynvelve]]. Twin sibling of [[Topsy]].

## Personality

- Hyperactive; comedic duo with Topsy (S01)
- Squirmy, quick-handed

## Key Events

- **[[Session 01 — Prisoners of the Underdark]]:** Introduced alongside Topsy. Kept Aeolus company.
- **[[Session 02 — Come Get Your Shit]]:** Attacked drow alongside Topsy during escape. [[Stool]] latched onto Turvy's back. Escaped into pool.

## Notes

- Taylor and Matt roll for Topsy and Turvy during combat
- Wererat nature (revealed later; not known in S01–S02)
- Reluctant to return to [[03-World-Lore/Locations/Blingdenstone]] — reason unknown
