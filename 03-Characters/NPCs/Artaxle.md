# Artaxle

Young drow from [[04-World-Lore/Locations/Velkynvelve]] garrison. [[Jorlan Duskryn]]'s protégé. Eyes flash purple (possible demonic corruption connection — same phenomenon observed in S01 barracks drow by [[Amanita de'Champignon]]).

## Description / Appearance

- Young drow male
- Eyes flash purple — same phenomenon Amanita noticed in S01 (a drow guard's eyes briefly turning from red to purple)
- Speaks halting Common mixed with Undercommon; Binks translates

## Backstory

- Stationed at Velkynvelve as part of the garrison
- Recognized by [[Amanita de'Champignon]] from S01 — the drow she spoke to in the barracks whose eyes flashed purple
- [[Jorlan Duskryn]] saw "the good in the young man" and placed his hope for drow reform in Artaxle

## Personality

- Shocked and worried after Jorlan's death
- Awed and terrified by Binks's desecration of the Shrine of Lolth
- Respectful of the party but chose duty over companionship

## Key Events

- **[[Session 14 — Far From the Sun|Session 14]]:** Called down to the party from above that Jorlan was dying. Led the party to Jorlan in the northern watchpost. After Jorlan's death, was downcast but resolved. Jorlan had given him a mission: return to [[Menzoberranzan]] with false intelligence blaming the demons (not the party) for Velkynvelve's fall. Declined [[Kit Aluri]]'s offer to join the party. Offered to guide the party to [[Sloobludop]] before splitting off toward Menzoberranzan. Reacted with shock to the shrine desecration: "You overcame the power of Lolth!"

## Relationships

- **[[Jorlan Duskryn]]:** Mentor/protégé. Artaxle tried to keep Jorlan alive. Jorlan placed his hope for drow reform in Artaxle.
- **[[Kit Aluri]]:** Kit extended friendship and an invitation to travel together. Artaxle declined but departed as an ally.
- **[[Amanita de'Champignon]]:** Recognized from S01 barracks. Greeted enthusiastically by Amanita despite the language barrier.

## Key Quotes

- "Jorlan's dying, he needs some help." — S14 (calling down in Undercommon) [Serious]
- "You overcame the power of Lolth. You are surely— oh, blaspheme!" — S14, after shrine desecration [Serious]
