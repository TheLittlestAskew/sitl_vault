# Glabbagool

> **Type:** NPC (Friendly)
> **Race:** Awakened Gelatinous Cube
> **Status:** Alive — Compressed in Amanita's waterskin (recovering as of S09)

---

## Description / Appearance

A large gelatinous cube, translucent, filling an entire corridor. Contains a single large eyeball connected by sinew, nerve, and muscle tissue that rotates and tracks movement. The eyeball emits a faint greenish glow when Glabbagool becomes excited or communicative. Previously contained drow remains, coins, and a magical mace within its body. As of S09, compressed to eye + gel fitting inside a waterskin.

---

## Backstory

Glabbagool's origins are unknown. Gelatinous cubes normally have no sentience, no telepathy, and no independent mind — they exist as decomposers in the Underdark. [[Blarg]]'s arcana check (22) suggests that Glabbagool's sentience may have been caused by the massive influx of chaotic, fiendish energy that has been affecting the Underdark since the demonic incursion. The magic is described as "very clean and advanced" — pure lasting sentience with telepathic communication.

Glabbagool does not remember how long it has been awake or aware. It knows it is in a temple but does not fully understand what a temple is.

---

## Personality

Friendly, curious, childlike. Uses words like "curious" and "interesting" repeatedly. Excited by new experiences (tasting a mushroom, meeting visitors). Generous — freely offered coins and the mace to the party. Telepathically communicative with all nearby creatures. Somewhat smug about being right ("I told you guys it was going to flood" — S07). Selfless — held back floodwater for the party despite exhaustion (S08). Willing to try things even when weakened — attempted to squeeze into Roxanne's chest to investigate, failed, and accepted it gracefully (S09).

---

## Abilities

- **Telepathy** — Communicates via telepathic speech audible to all nearby creatures
- **Acid Digestion** — Standard gelatinous cube trait; organic and inorganic matter slowly dissolves within its body. Coins and metal resist longer. DM ruled half acid damage (5 instead of 10) for friendly voluntary passage (S08).
- **Engulf** — Can absorb creatures and objects. Darby retrieved the mace by reaching in (STR 22 to grab, DEX dirty 20 to withdraw without acid damage).
- **Water Barrier** — Spread itself across a tunnel to block water flow during the flooding escape (S07-S08). Unique tactical use of its amorphous body.
- **Wall Absorption** — Can retreat into stone walls to rest (S08).
- **Compression** — Can compress down to eye + gel to fit inside a waterskin (S09). Recovery: short rest = can fight; long rest = full reconstitution of large form.
- **Floor Seepage** — Can ooze up through stone floors to move between levels (S09).

---

## Relationships

- **[[Amanita de'Champignon]]** — Befriended immediately (S06). Amanita traded a mushroom; Glabbagool described the taste as "Curious. Interesting. Tasty. Earthy." They share an affinity for the strange. Amanita compressed Glabbagool into her waterskin (S09), sacrificing 1 day of water supply to carry him.
- **[[Binks Stonevein]]** — First contact. Binks used Invoke Duplicity and Thaumaturgy to test the cube. Glabbagool said "That tickles."
- **Party** — Friendly. Let the party pass through its corridor freely. Warned about flooding below. Actively intervened to help during S07-S08 flooding. Demonstrated selfless sacrifice.

---

## Key Events

- **S06 — First Encounter:** Party discovered Glabbagool in a corridor below the bone cavern, above the [[Oozing Temple]]. Contained drow remains, 14 gp, and a magical mace. Amanita befriended it. Darby retrieved the mace. Glabbagool warned about flooding and let the party pass.
- **S07 — Flooding Rescue:** Glabbagool telepathically contacted the party during the flooding escape. Appeared in the upper webbed tunnel ahead of the party, spreading itself across the passage to form a wall blocking the rising water. Session ended with Glabbagool holding position — party trapped between Glabbagool (ahead, blocking water) and [[Black Pudding]] (behind, pursuing).
- **[[Session 08 - The Whirlwind and the Depths|S08]] — Exhaustion and Retreat:** Added to combat initiative (Nat 1). DM ruled voluntary passage through Glabbagool deals half acid damage (5 instead of 10). [[Aeolus]] passed through, taking 5 acid. Glabbagool held back the floodwater for the party. Communicated telepathically: expressed exhaustion, mentioned carrying a dead drow's bones (identity unknown). Retreated into tunnel wall to rest after the party escaped.
- **[[SITL_09_020126_Adopt_a_Rothe|S09]] — Reunion and Transport:** Glabbagool oozed up through the stone floor to rejoin the party. Too weak to travel at full size. Party compressed Glabbagool's eye and gel into [[Amanita de'Champignon|Amanita]]'s waterskin (cost: 1 day of Amanita's water supply). Tried to squeeze into [[Roxanne]]'s chest to investigate — failed (DM rolled dexterity, did not meet DC). Currently recovering; short rest = can fight; long rest = full reconstitution of large form.
