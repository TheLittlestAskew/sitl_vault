# Naos

> **Type:** NPC (Archfey Patron)
> **Race:** Archfey
> **Status:** Endangered — avatar dissolved, real form infiltrating the corrupted entity
> **Affiliation:** [[Aeolus]]'s Archfey Patron; Court of Stars

---

## Description / Appearance

Appeared to Aeolus as a humanoid figure sitting on a mossy stone, fiddling with a wedding band. Physical avatar dissolved into spores after giving Aeolus the Ring of Spell Storing. The avatar was "only a portion of my power" — his real form is elsewhere.

## Backstory

Per Naos's exposition (S11): Naos is an Archfey who fell in love with Aeolus on the material plane. He bonded them together magically to gain power, but did so without Aeolus's knowledge or consent. He learned of a threat — a corrupted counterpart of [[Psilofyr]], a female entity from the Abyss who seeks to control death itself. Naos attempted to infiltrate this entity alone but failed and is now in danger, needing rescue.

## Personality

Romantic, remorseful, self-sacrificing. Kneeled when offering the ring. Expressed genuine love: "I never wanted to leave you. It was one of the hardest things that I've ever done in all the time that I've been alive." Acknowledged wrongdoing in bonding Aeolus without consent. Willing to dissolve his avatar to give Aeolus the power he needs.

## Abilities & Powers

- Created the patron-warlock bond with Aeolus
- Avatar form on the material plane and in the Feydark
- Ring of Spell Storing — Naos's essence flooded into the ring before his avatar dissolved
- Infiltration of cosmic-level threats (attempted; failed)

## Key Events

- **[[Session 11 — Gifts_of_the_Carrion_King|Session 11]]:** Appeared in Aeolus's dream/vision in the Feydark. Sat on a mossy stone with the wedding ring. Delivered lengthy exposition about his motives, the corrupted entity, and his current danger. Kneeled and offered the ring to Aeolus. Aeolus accepted. Naos's essence flooded into the ring. His avatar dissolved into spores. [[Echo]] landed and pecked at the ring: "So dramatic. What a romantic."

## Key Quotes

- "If any part of me remains after all of this is through, I would love to spend the rest of it with you. If you'll have me." — S11 [Serious/Important]

## Relationships

- **[[Aeolus]]:** Patron. Fell in love with Aeolus, bonded them without consent. Now asks for rescue. The Ring of Spell Storing is both a magical tool and a wedding band.
- **[[Echo]]:** Connected to Naos's patronage of Aeolus. Echo mocked Naos's dramatic dissolution: "So dramatic."
- **[[Psilofyr]]:** Allied against the corrupted entity. Naos and Psilofyr share a common enemy.
- **The Corrupted Entity:** Naos infiltrated her and is now trapped/endangered. She is female, from the Abyss, and seeks to control death and rot.
