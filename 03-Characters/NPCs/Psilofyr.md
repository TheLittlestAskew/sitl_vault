# Psilofyr

> **Type:** NPC (Archfey / Deity Figure)
> **Also Known As:** The Carrion King, Archfey of Myconids
> **Status:** Active — remained in the Feydark when party departed (S11)
> **Affiliation:** Court of Stars (Feydark)

---

## Description / Appearance

Reconstituted from the corpse of a Myconid Sovereign after the party's trial (S10). Grew larger, more humanoid, more deliberate in form. Spoke telepathically to all party members simultaneously. In S11, shifted into a casual persona when speaking to Amanita — described as wearing a "mushroom wizard cap," using bro-ish language.

## Backstory

Per Echo's exposition (S10): Psilofyr is the Archfey of Myconids. His realm is in the [[04-World-Lore/Locations/The Feydark|Feydark]], near the Court of Stars. He creates avatars that develop independent minds and personalities — some become evil. The party dealt with one such avatar during the trial. The actual "resource does not go into the Feydark at all" — this is DM Addison's homebrew expansion.

Per [[Blarg]] History check (16, S10): Known as the Carrion King. Stories and lore exist about Psilofyr among dwarven and druidic traditions.

Per S11 exposition: Psilofyr warned of a corrupted counterpart — a female entity from the Abyss who seeks to control death and rot. A twisted reflection of Psilofyr. [[Naos]] also warned of this entity. Psilofyr is invested in the party as champions against this threat.

## Personality

Ancient, patient, authoritative. Addresses the party collectively as "friend" (singular). Forced the party unconscious with no saving throw (S10). In S11, showed range: solemn and weighty when granting Fey Touched, but shifted to a laid-back "bro" persona when speaking to Amanita ("You're right, dude. It's a super bummer." / "I love that about you. You're going to kick some ass."). The casual shift surprised the table but felt authentic to Psilofyr's connection with Amanita.

## Abilities & Powers

- Telepathic communication (mass, all party members simultaneously)
- Override Constitution saving throws — forced all PCs unconscious (no save, S10)
- Controls the Feydark environment — fog, visions, time dilation
- Creates avatars (Myconid Sovereigns) that develop independent minds
- Empowers trinkets with magical properties (growth items)
- Grants forced long rest via spore explosion
- Grants feats (Fey Touched) — burning roots wrapped around PCs' arms (S11)
- Resurrected [[Topsy]] by retrieving her body from the sealed safe house via mycelial network (S11)
- Created the Nexus Pools — seven portals to different Underdark/surface locations (S11)

## Key Events

- **[[Session 10 — 021526_Echoes_in_the_Feydark|Session 10]]:** Party entered the Feydark. Myconid Sovereign + 5 Adults attacked as trial. After defeat, Sovereign reconstituted into Psilofyr's avatar. Spoke: "You have shown your potential. Well done, friend. We will speak soon. But now you must rest." Forced long rest via spore explosion. Empowered Darby's trinket and Binks's loot pick. Did NOT interact with or empower Kit.
- **[[Session 11 — Gifts_of_the_Carrion_King|Session 11]]:** Orchestrated individual dream/trance sequences for all PCs. Kit: scripted nightmare → Doll of the Fallen Soldier. Aeolus: Naos reunion → Ring of Spell Storing. Blarg: Chris/goblins → enhanced carving. Amanita: deep trance → enhanced pipe + warpick + four future paths. Darby: totem empowerment. Binks: grandfather voice → enhanced loot pick. Resurrected [[Topsy]] via mycelial fibers. Granted all PCs **Fey Touched** feat. Led party to **Nexus Pools** (seven portals). Said farewell. Confirmed portals are one-way — party cannot return to the Feydark via the same route.
