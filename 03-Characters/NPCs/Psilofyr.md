# Psilofyr

> **Type:** NPC (Archfey / Deity Figure)
> **Also Known As:** The Carrion King, Archfey of Myconids
> **Status:** Active — spoke telepathically to party (S10)
> **Affiliation:** Court of Stars (Feydark)

---

## Description / Appearance

Reconstituted from the corpse of a Myconid Sovereign after the party's trial. Grew larger, more humanoid, more deliberate in form. Spoke telepathically to all party members simultaneously.

## Backstory

Per Echo's exposition (S10): Psilofyr is the Archfey of Myconids. His realm is in the [[04-World-Lore/Locations/The Feydark|Feydark]], near the Court of Stars. He creates avatars that develop independent minds and personalities — some become evil. The party dealt with one such avatar during the trial. The actual "resource does not go into the Feydark at all" — this is DM Addison's homebrew expansion.

Per [[Blarg]] History check (16, S10): Known as the Carrion King. Stories and lore exist about Psilofyr among dwarven and druidic traditions.

Per [[Amanita de'Champignon]] History check (15, S10): Archfey of Myconids — confirmed via advantage roll.

## Personality

Ancient, patient, authoritative. Speaks in declarative statements. "You have shown your potential. Well done, friend. We will speak soon. But now you must rest." Addresses the party collectively as "friend" (singular). Forced the party unconscious with no saving throw — "You're now dealing with an archfey" (DM).

## Abilities & Powers

- Telepathic communication (mass, all party members simultaneously)
- Override Constitution saving throws — forced all PCs unconscious (no save)
- Controls the Feydark environment — fog, visions, time dilation
- Creates avatars (Myconid Sovereigns) that develop independent minds
- Empowers trinkets with magical properties
- Grants forced long rest via spore explosion

## Key Events

- **[[SITL_10_021526_Echoes_in_the_Feydark|Session 10]]:** Party entered the Feydark and experienced personalized visions orchestrated by Psilofyr. A Myconid Sovereign + 5 Adults attacked as a trial. After the party defeated the Sovereign, it reconstituted into Psilofyr's avatar form. Psilofyr spoke: "You have shown your potential. Well done, friend. We will speak soon. But now you must rest." Forced the party unconscious with a spore explosion. Granted long rest + level-up to 4. Empowered Darby's trinket and Binks's loot pick. Did NOT interact with or empower Kit.
