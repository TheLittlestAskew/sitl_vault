# Garruk

Drow crossbowman from [[04-World-Lore/Locations/Velkynvelve]] garrison. One of the last surviving drow combatants after the Battle of Velkynvelve (S12-S14).

## Description / Appearance

- Drow male
- Crossbow sniper — was shooting from shadows during the post-battle standoff

## Key Events

- **[[Session 14 — Far From the Sun|Session 14]]:** During Kit's Persuasion speech, the drow elite ordered Garruk to drop his weapon. He complied initially but when [[Asha Vandree]]'s Command (Halt) expired and she cast Darkness, Garruk also fled into the darkness. Before the Darkness cloud dissipated, [[Binks Stonevein]] briefly spotted a massive riding lizard (Shoor's) skittering across the cavern wall into the cloud (perception 22). When the darkness dropped, Garruk was gone — along with [[Ilvara Mizzrym]]'s body and Shoor's riding lizard. Kit chose not to pursue: "I got better things to do."

## Relationships

- **Drow Elite (unnamed):** Followed the elite's orders to stand down, then fled when opportunity arose
- **[[Ilvara Mizzrym]]:** Took her body when fleeing — likely to return it to Menzoberranzan

## Significance

If Garruk reaches [[Menzoberranzan]] before [[Artaxle]], the drow will know the truth about what happened at Velkynvelve and who was responsible. This creates a race between Artaxle's false intel and Garruk's true account — a ticking clock for the party.
