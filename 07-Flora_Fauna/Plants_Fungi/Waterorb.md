# Waterorb

> **Type:** Water-containing Underdark fungus
> **First Encountered:** [[SITL_15_050326_The_Long_Road_to_Sloobludop|Session 15]]
> **Identified By:** [[Amanita de'Champignon]]

---

## Description

An Underdark fungus that contains water, identified by [[Amanita de'Champignon]] in the Fungal Cavern on Day 4 of travel to Sloobludop. Functions as a natural water source in the Underdark — useful for refilling waterskins during long travel.

## Properties

- Contains drinkable water
- Can be used to refill waterskins
- No known dangerous properties

## Party Interaction

- Identified in the [[Fungal Cavern (Day 4)]] alongside [[Timmask]], [[Tongue of Madness]], and [[Nightlight]].
- Party likely refilled waterskins (DM mentioned the option after 4 days of travel).
