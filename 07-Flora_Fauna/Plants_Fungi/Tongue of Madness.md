# Tongue of Madness

> **Type:** Underdark fungus (poisonous)
> **First Encountered:** [[SITL_15_050326_The_Long_Road_to_Sloobludop|Session 15]]
> **Identified By:** [[Amanita de'Champignon]]

---

## Description

An Underdark fungus identified by [[Amanita de'Champignon]] in the Fungal Cavern on Day 4 of travel to Sloobludop. Not damaging on contact, but poisonous if consumed. Could potentially be used to poison someone.

## Properties

- Not damaging to step on or touch
- Poisonous if eaten
- Potential use as a poison agent

## Party Interaction

- Identified in the [[Fungal Cavern (Day 4)]] alongside [[Timmask]], Nightlight, and [[Waterorb]].
- Amanita guided party to avoid it.
- Not harvested.
