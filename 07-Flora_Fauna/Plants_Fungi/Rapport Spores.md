# Rapport Spores

## Description
Telepathic spore clouds produced by myconids. When released, rapport spores create a temporary telepathic link between all creatures within the cloud's range, allowing silent communication regardless of shared language.

## Properties
- **Telepathic communication:** Creates mental link between creatures in range
- **No audible speech:** Communication is entirely mental
- **Range:** Limited area around the myconid (exact range not specified in campaign)
- **Duration:** Temporary; requires proximity to the myconid

## Campaign Appearances

### [[Session 01 — Prisoners of the Underdark]]
- [[Stool]] used rapport spores to commune with [[Amanita de'Champignon]] in the prison cell
- Bridged [[Darby Stonefeather]]'s first telepathic conversation — Darby waved at Amanita, and Stool sent spores to Darby to connect them
- Stool identified [[Neverlight Grove]] in Amanita's dream through the spore link

### [[Session 02 — Come Get Your Shit]]
- Amanita's spore-sensing extended across the cavern via lichen/fungus colonial relationship
- She sensed the vrocks/chasmes as fiendish/demonic through this fungal network connection

### [[Session 05 — Silvered]]
- Stool communicated about mushrooms below the safe house floor and Neverlight Grove's distance through the spore network

## Notes
- Rapport spores are the primary way [[Stool]] communicates — myconid sprouts do not have audible speech
- Amanita's Circle of Spores druid subclass gives her a special affinity with these spores
- The fungal network beneath the safe house floor (S05) may be connected to myconid biology
