# Underdark Bioluminescent Fungi

## Description
The Underdark is home to vast ecosystems of bioluminescent fungi, mosses, lichens, and mycelium networks that provide dim illumination in the otherwise lightless caverns. These organisms cover floors, walls, and ceilings in wide swaths, creating what Addison described as an "underground rainforest."

## Properties
- **Bioluminescence:** Provides dim natural light in the Underdark
- **Variety:** "Fruiting fungal bodies of all shapes, sizes, and colors" (Addison's S03 recap)
- **Mycelium networks:** Strings of mycelium run through rock and soil, connecting distant fungal colonies
- **Moisture:** The areas rich in fungi tend to be damp with water trickling down walls; humid air

- Faint purple phosphorescence and bioluminescent veins in the rock at [[04-World-Lore/Locations/Velkynvelve]]

- "Various bioluminescent strings of mycelium dimly illuminating the way"

### [[Session 05 — Silvered]]
- [[Amanita de'Champignon]] detected a circular mycelial network running beneath the safe house floor (Investigation 19)
- [[Stool]] confirmed fungal presence below, connected to something deeper
- When Amanita struck the floor with the mithral pickaxe, spores erupted and the cavity below was filled with mycelium
- Three tunnel passages branched off below, all threaded with fungal growth

## Notes
- The fungal networks appear to be interconnected across vast distances; Amanita's spore-sensing and Stool's rapport spores can tap into these networks
- The [[faerzress]] may interact with or be channeled through fungal networks (the safe house's corrupted faerzress and the mycelium below were in the same location)
- This campaign features heavy fungi/plant content given the Circle of Spores druid (Amanita) and myconid companion (Stool)
