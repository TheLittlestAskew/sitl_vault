# Ormu

> **Type:** Bioluminescent green moss
> **First Encountered:** [[Session 15 — The Long Road to Sloobludop|Session 15]]
> **Discovered By:** [[Blarg]] (Nature 23)

---

## Description

Bioluminescent green moss that sheds dim light in a 5-foot radius. Found growing on Underdark tunnel walls during the party's travel from Velkynvelve to Sloobludop (Days 1–2).

## Properties

- Sheds dim light in 5-foot radius naturally
- Can be harvested, dried, and made into a phosphorescent powder or pigment
- Applied to a creature or object: functions as **Faerie Fire** (creature outlined in light, advantage on attack rolls against it)
- Very hard to remove once applied
- If eaten, makes your tongue glow
- Requires ~2 days of drying before it can be converted to powder form

## Uses

- **Offensive:** Throw powder at enemies to outline them (improvised Faerie Fire)
- **Defensive:** Apply to allies or objects for visibility in dark environments
- **Utility:** Marking system — trail markers, signaling, identifying targets
- **Cosmetic:** Bioluminescent pigment

## Party Interaction

- [[Blarg]] discovered Ormu while foraging for exotic fungi (Nature 23). Harvested with his sickle.
- [[Amanita de'Champignon]] recognized it: "If you eat it, your tongue glows."
- Currently drying; will be ready for powder form ~Day 3–4 of travel.
