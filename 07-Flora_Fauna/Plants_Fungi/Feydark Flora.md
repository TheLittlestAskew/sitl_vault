# Feydark Fungi & Flora

> **Type:** Fungal / Plant organisms
> **Location:** [[04-World-Lore/Locations/The Feydark]]
> **First Encountered:** Session 10

---

## Description

The Feydark's plant life is distinct from standard Underdark fungi. The environment blends underground fungal ecosystems with fae-touched surface-like vegetation.

### Observed Flora (S10)

- **Spongy lichen and mud:** Ground cover at the Feydark arrival zone. Wet, warm, yielding underfoot.
- **Massive root systems:** Enormous roots visible along Roxanne's path. Comparable to ancient tree roots but underground.
- **Autumnal leaves:** Leaves in autumn colors — reds, oranges, browns. Present despite being underground. Likely fae illusion or fae-touched real vegetation.
- **Ferns:** Dripping with moisture along Roxanne's path.
- **Bioluminescent fungi:** Present throughout; different species from standard Underdark bioluminescent fungi. Warmer tones, more vibrant.
- **Atmospheric spores:** Active spores in the air. Constitution saves required on arrival. Psilofyr's spore explosion forced the party unconscious at session end.
- **Feydark-specific mushroom species:** Not yet identified or cataloged. Amanita's expertise may provide more detail in future sessions.

### Connection to Mycelial Network

The Feydark portal in the Fungal Pond was described as "connected to the fungal network" (Blarg's Arcana 23, S09). The Feydark's flora appears to be part of a larger mycelial network that spans the Underdark and connects to Psilofyr's domain.
