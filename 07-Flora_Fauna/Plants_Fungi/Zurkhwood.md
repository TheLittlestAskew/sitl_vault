# Zurkhwood

## Description
Giant mushroom wood native to the Underdark. Zurkhwood is used as a primary building material throughout the Underdark, serving the same role that timber does on the surface. It is edible.

## Properties
- **Structural material:** Used to build platforms, bridges, furniture, chests, and the spider statue at [[Velkynvelve]]
- **Edible:** Identified as edible by [[Blarg]] (Nature check 26, S01)
- **Appearance:** Woody mushroom material; strong enough for construction

## Campaign Appearances

### [[Session 01 — Prisoners of the Underdark]]
- Blarg identified the platform material at Velkynvelve as Zurkhwood with a Nature 26 check — "genuinely delighted" at the discovery
- The 10ft spider statue in the Shrine to Lolth is carved from Zurkhwood
- Bridges and platforms at Velkynvelve are made from Zurkhwood planks and silk cord
- Binks's foot broke through a Zurkhwood plank during stealth approach (S02)

### [[Session 02 — Come Get Your Shit]]
- Chests in the barracks and elite barracks were Zurkhwood
- Darby carried 1-2 Zurkhwood chests of equipment during the escape

### [[Session 03 — Into the Dark]]
- Zurkhwood and other fungi covered the mushroom cavern walls, floors, and ceilings in the passages north of Velkynvelve

## Spelling Note
DM Addison verbally spelled it "C-U-R-K-H wood" in S01. Canonical spelling per published D&D sources is **Zurkhwood**. Addison's written recaps use "zhirkwood" in one instance. Use "Zurkhwood" consistently.
