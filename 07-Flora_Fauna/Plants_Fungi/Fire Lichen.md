# Fire Lichen

## Description
Pale orange-white lichen found growing on stone surfaces in geothermally heated Underdark caverns. Identified by [[Amanita de'Champignon]] during the S03 mushroom cavern passage.

## Properties
- **Appearance:** Pale orange-white coloration on surrounding stone
- **Habitat:** Grows near geothermal vents; associated with steam plumes from the stone
- **Edible:** A known food source in the Underdark (per D&D source material)
- **Co-occurrence:** Found alongside [[Timmask]] (devil's mushroom) and [[Fire Beetle]] colonies

## Campaign Appearances

### [[Session 03 — Into the Dark]]
- Observed in the mushroom cavern north of [[Velkynvelve]]. Fire beetles were feeding on both the lichen and the Timmask toadstools.
- No direct interaction by the party; identified visually by Amanita.
