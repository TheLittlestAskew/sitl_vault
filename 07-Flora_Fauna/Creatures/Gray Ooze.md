# Gray Ooze

## Description
Enormous ooze inhabiting the pool at the base of the [[Velkynvelve]] cavern. Slimy and slick on the rock and sand bottom.

## Abilities Observed
- Telepathic broadcast: "FLESH! Flesh for the Faceless Lord!" — first reference to Juiblex (demon lord of oozes) in the campaign
- Elongated blob-arm attack: hit [[Amanita de'Champignon]] hard, left acidic ooze to eat away at clothes and flesh
- Slick surface caused [[Darby Stonefeather]] to slip

## Encounters

### [[Session 03 — Into the Dark]]
- Triggered when [[Darby Stonefeather]] slipped on it while walking along the pool bottom carrying two Zurkhwood chests
- Telepathically broadcast "Flesh for the Faceless Lord" to the entire party
- Amanita sent Chill Touch and Halo of Spores; ooze retaliated with massive arm strike, hurting Amanita badly
- Kit pulled Darby away from the ooze to safety
- Darby lashed it with her whip (9 damage)
- [[Blarg]] swam to Amanita, healed her (Cure Wounds 2nd level, 23 HP), then sent a bolt of radiant light into the ooze's central mass — killing it
- Ooze denatured and sank into the depths

## Significance
- First indication of Juiblex's influence in the Underdark
- "Faceless Lord" = Juiblex, demon lord of oozes and slimes
- Connected to the broader demon lord threat established by the vrock/chasme appearance at Velkynvelve
