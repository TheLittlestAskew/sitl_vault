# Quaggoth

## Description
Large, ape-like Underdark creatures. Typically aggressive and territorial. Used as laborers and muscle by the drow at [[04-World-Lore/Locations/Velkynvelve]]. The Quaggoth Den was the worst duty assignment for prisoners.

## Notable Individuals
- **[[Derendil]]** — A quaggoth prisoner who wears clothing, speaks Elvish, and claims to be a polymorphed elven prince. His mannerisms are "very much not quaggoth-like." Captured by other quaggoths during the S02 escape.

## Encounters

### [[Session 01 — Prisoners of the Underdark]]
- Quaggoths observed in the den area of Velkynvelve
- Used by drow as laborers; operated the platform lift alongside prisoners
- Darby cranked the lift solo, outperforming the quaggoths (strength 19)

### [[Session 02 — Come Get Your Shit]]
- Kit's first combat action: greatsword vs quaggoth (19 to hit, 11 damage)
- Blarg's Produce Flame dealt 1 fire damage to a quaggoth
- Aeolus's Eldritch Blast missed a quaggoth
- Amanita pulled a quaggoth fang from a guard's back and dipped it in drow poison as an improvised weapon
- Quaggoths rushed from their den during the escape chaos; captured [[Derendil]]

## Notes
- Quaggoths at Velkynvelve served the drow, not the demons
- Derendil is the notable exception to typical quaggoth behavior
