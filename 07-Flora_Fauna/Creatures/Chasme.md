# Chasme

## Description
Fly-like demons that chased the [[Vrock]] into [[04-World-Lore/Locations/Velkynvelve]]. Multiple chasmes were present during the demon incursion.

## Abilities Observed
- No direct combat with the party observed; fought drow guards on the outpost platforms

## Encounters

### [[Session 01 — Prisoners of the Underdark]] / [[Session 02 — Come Get Your Shit]]
- Multiple chasmes chased a vrock from the northern passage into Velkynvelve
- Fought alongside/against the vrock and drow guards
- The combined demon incursion created the chaos that enabled the party's escape

## Significance
- Part of the broader demon lord incursion into the Underdark
- Multiple demons appearing together suggests organized demonic activity
