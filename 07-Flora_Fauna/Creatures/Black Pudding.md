# Black Pudding

> **Type:** Ooze
> **Classification:** Large ooze
> **Threat Level:** High (at party level 3)
> **First Appeared:** [[Session 07 — Escape from the Oozing Temple]]
> **Defeated:** [[Session 08 - The Whirlwind and the Depths|Session 08]]

---

## Description / Appearance

A massive black ooze that rose from the flooded trapdoor pits in the [[Oozing Temple]]. Described as "acid and hunger given shape" in Kit's journal. Dark, amorphous, capable of extending pseudopods to attack.

---

## Abilities Observed

- **Pseudopod Attack:** Lashed out at [[Darby Stonefeather]] with a pseudopod (rolled 15 vs AC 16 — missed by 1, S07). Hit [[Kit Aluri]] for 8 bludgeoning + 17 acid (S08), dropping Kit to 0 HP.
- **Corrosive Form:** Acid damage on contact. Permanently degraded Kit's armor by -1 AC (S08). DM ruled this accumulates per hit; armor destroyed at AC 10.
- **Split:** When struck with slashing damage (Kit's greatsword, S08), the pudding split into two halves. Slashing damage has no effect on Black Pudding — mass simply reforms. Bludgeoning and radiant confirmed effective.
- **Aquatic Movement:** Rose with the flooding water from the trapdoor pits, suggesting it can move through or float in water.

---

## Behavior

- Emerged from a flooded trapdoor pit at the end of Round 2 during the environmental escape encounter (S07).
- Pursued the party through the narrow tunnel from behind.
- Did not appear to be intelligent or communicative (unlike [[Glabbagool]]).
- After splitting, both halves continued fighting independently.

---

## Encounters

- **S07 — Flooding Escape:** Rose from flooded pit. Attacked Darby (pseudopod, 15 vs AC 16 — miss). Session ended with the Black Pudding pursuing the party from behind while [[Glabbagool]] blocked water from ahead. Encounter unresolved.
- **[[Session 08 - The Whirlwind and the Depths|S08]] — Final Battle:** Combat continued from S07. Kit used Trip Attack to knock it prone (first Battle Master maneuver use). Kit's greatsword (slashing) caused pudding to split — DM confirmed slashing is ineffective, mass reforms. Pudding hit Kit for 25 total damage (8 bludgeoning + 17 acid), dropping her to 0 HP; Kit returned to 1 HP via Relentless Endurance. Blarg's Moonbeam dealt 30 total radiant damage across two failed Con saves. Darby's first spectral rage (whirlwind) parted the floodwater. Amanita's Poison Spray dealt 9 damage. **Half 1** destroyed by combined opportunity attacks from Kit (Nat 20 crit, 19 damage) and Darby (13 damage with Religion-granted D6 radiant bonus). **Half 2** destroyed by Darby's killing blow (12 damage with rage bonus). Both halves defeated; encounter resolved.

---

## DM Rulings (S08)

- Slashing damage has no effect on Black Pudding — mass reforms after being cut
- Bludgeoning and radiant damage confirmed effective
- Trip Attack (Battle Master maneuver) can knock a Black Pudding prone — it failed its Str save vs. Kit's DC
- Splitting triggered by Kit's greatsword slashing damage; each half fought independently

---

## Notes

- The Black Pudding may have been lurking in the trapdoor pits since the party first traversed the [[Oozing Temple]] in S06. Kit's POV journal from S06 mentions "something black and hungry at the bottom" of a pit that Amanita nearly fell into.
- Standard Black Puddings in D&D 5E are CR 4 — a significant threat to a level 3 party.
- Not to be confused with the gray oozes encountered in S03, S06, and S08, which are smaller and less dangerous.
- Kit's armor degradation is the first permanent equipment loss in the campaign.
