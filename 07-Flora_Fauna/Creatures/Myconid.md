# Myconid (Sovereign & Adult)

> **Type:** Plant creature
> **Classification:** Myconid
> **First Encountered:** Session 10 — Feydark trial
> **Affiliation:** [[Psilofyr]] (avatars/servants)

---

## Myconid Sovereign

### Description
Larger than Myconid Adults. Reconstituted into a larger, more humanoid form after death — revealed as an avatar of [[Psilofyr]].

### Abilities Observed (S10)
- **Multi-attack:** Fist + Pacifying Spores in a single turn
- **Fist:** Bludgeoning damage
- **Pacifying Spores:** Constitution save or fall asleep. Binks and Blarg both saved.
- **Dying Charm (telepathic):** Upon death, attempted to charm [[Amanita de'Champignon]] — "Creature of the many, help us." Amanita saved (Wis 17, elf advantage vs. charm).
- **Reconstitution:** Corpse reconstituted into larger, more humanoid form after death. Psilofyr spoke through it.

### Combat Stats (estimated from S10)
- **AC:** ~13 (hit by 17, 18; unclear lower bound)
- **HP:** High — took 19 (Witch Bolt) + 3 (Moonbeam) + 11 (Moonbeam) + 13 (Kit's killing blow) = ~46+ before death
- **Con Save:** 14 (saved vs Moonbeam DC 14 round 1; failed round 2)

### Death
Killed by [[Kit Aluri]] — greatsword + Trip Attack, leaping off bear-[[Blarg]]'s back. 13 damage. Reconstituted into Psilofyr avatar.

---

## Myconid Adult

### Description
Tall, silent mushroom humanoids. Blended into roots and leaves in the treeline. Five encountered (labeled A-E).

### Abilities Observed (S10)
- **Fist:** Bludgeoning damage (5 base) + poison damage (5). Nat 20 crit dealt 10 bludgeoning + 5 poison = 15 total (vs Amanita).
- **AC:** 12 (Darby missed with 10, hit with 17)
- **HP:** Low-medium — Myconid A killed by 10 + 9 + 12 damage; Myconid E killed by 18 damage crit

### Notable Behaviors
- **Myconid C stood down:** Convinced by [[Stool]]'s rapport spores (persuasion 16 with Amanita advantage). Kneeled and said "Welcome, one of the many."
- **Myconid A rolled nat 1** vs Kit — DM gave Kit a free Riposte attack.
- **Myconid D** gored by [[Roxanne]] for 7 piercing, then hit by Darby for 14 damage.
- **Myconid E** destroyed by Blarg (bear) — nat 20 claw for 18 damage.
