# Giant Spider

## Description
Large spider inhabiting the Shrine to [[Lolth]] at [[04-World-Lore/Locations/Velkynvelve]]. Served as a guardian of the spider statue and its gemstone eyes.

## Abilities Observed
- Bite attack: 2 piercing + 9 poison damage (dealt to [[Aeolus]], S01)
- Appeared immune or highly resistant to poison damage (Blarg's Poison Spray dealt only 1 damage, S01)
- AC 14 (Aeolus's Shocking Grasp of 14 "just hit," S02)

## Encounters

### [[Session 01 — Prisoners of the Underdark]]
- Triggered when [[Aeolus]]'s bat familiar extracted a gemstone from the spider statue's eye
- [[Blarg]] and [[Aeolus]] fought the spider in the shrine
- Blarg's Poison Spray: 1 damage (spider appeared immune to poison). Second Poison Spray: 3 damage
- Aeolus's Eldritch Blast: 7 force damage
- Spider bit Aeolus: 11 damage (2 piercing + 9 poison), approximately half HP
- Combat in progress at session end; spider was bloodied

### [[Session 02 — Come Get Your Shit]]
- Combat continued from S01
- Aeolus used Shocking Grasp (14 to hit, 4 lightning damage; disengaged safely)
- Spider targeted Aeolus specifically even during vrock/chasme chaos — likely connected to the Lolth curse
- Blarg wild shaped into wolf; bite missed (roll 5)
- Aeolus dragged wolf-Blarg out of shrine
- DM noted: ranged spell attacks within 5ft should have had disadvantage; waived for S01, enforced from S02

## Notes
- The spider's continued targeting of Aeolus during the demon chaos suggests it was magically compelled by the Lolth curse, not acting on normal predatory instinct
- Giant spiders are sacred to Lolth and commonly found in drow settlements
