The Underdark is described in Forgotten Realms material as including drow cavern-cities, aboleth strongholds, mind flayer realms, and other lightless subterranean domains; even summarized sources emphasize that known locations are only a sampling of the “Realms Below.”

Menzoberranzan is the most important location here if you’re connecting Drizzt, Lolth, Jarlaxle, House Baenre, and MTG’s D&D crossover flavor. D&D Beyond’s Menzoberranzan feature identifies House Baenre as the longest-lasting ruling house in the city, which lines up with the city’s role as the signature drow political center.
