# House Rules & Rulings

DM Addison's rulings override all published rules. This page tracks confirmed homebrew and table decisions.

---

## Confirmed Rulings

### Combat Mechanics
- **Ranged spell attacks within 5ft:** DM waived disadvantage for S01 (Aeolus's Eldritch Blast vs spider). Enforced from S02 onward. Standard RAW applies going forward.
- **Wild Shape speech (2024 Rules):** Characters in beast form can speak normally. Discovered during S02 when Blarg wild shaped into wolf. This is per 2024 PHB rules, not homebrew.
- **Slashing damage vs. Black Pudding:** Confirmed S08 — slashing damage has no effect on Black Pudding (mass reforms). Bludgeoning and radiant confirmed effective. This triggered the pudding splitting into two halves after Kit's greatsword strike.
- **Friendly passage through Glabbagool:** DM ruled half acid damage (5 instead of 10) for allies passing through Glabbagool voluntarily (S08).
- **Trip Attack vs. oozes:** Battle Master Trip Attack maneuver can knock a Black Pudding prone (S08). Pudding failed Str save vs. Kit's DC.
- **Darby's mace damage upgrade:** DM upgraded Darby's mace extra damage from +1 flat to D4, and allowed radiant damage during S08 encounter (normally fire). DM may alternate between fire and radiant.
- **Crit bonus movement (Rule of Cool, S10):** When Blarg (bear form) rolled nat 20 on a claw attack against a nearly-dead myconid (3 HP remaining, 18 damage dealt), DM converted the overkill damage into bonus movement (15ft extra). Precedent set for rewarding cinematic crits.
- **Wild Shape movement (S10):** If you use all your movement and then wild shape, your movement does NOT reset. If you wild shape first, your movement changes to the beast form's speed. You cannot double-dip movement by transforming mid-turn.
- **Riposte on enemy nat 1 (S10):** DM gave Kit a free attack of opportunity (equivalent to Riposte) when a myconid rolled a nat 1 against her, even though Kit said she doesn't have Riposte as a maneuver. Treated as a DM gift/rule of cool.
- **Darby's mace as Green Flame Blade (S10):** DM clarified the mace is a common magic item that essentially grants Green Flame Blade as an action — erupts in green flame and adds extra 1d4 fire damage.
- **Action Surge timing in scripted encounters (S11):** Kit asked to Action Surge before the soldiers' multi-attacks; DM said she needed to declare it earlier. In the context of the nightmare (scripted/unwinnable), this ruling stands. Normal encounters should follow RAW (Action Surge usable on your turn).
- **Relentless Endurance suppression (S11):** During Kit's scripted nightmare, Relentless Endurance did NOT trigger when Kit was reduced to 0 HP. DM narration: "it does not happen." This is specific to the dream context — Relentless Endurance works normally in real combat.

### Lycanthropy
- **Wererats have control over their transformations (homebrew):** DM corrected in S05 — wererats normally have much more control than werewolves over their shifts. Topsy and Turvy's transformation in S04 was involuntary and abnormal, caused by corrupted faerzress. This is a DM homebrew distinction.
- **Moon influence on wererats (homebrew):** Wererats are still affected by the moon per DM ruling (S05). The moon flask given to Turvy channels lunar energy to help control changes.

### Exploration & Skills
- **Dungeon Delver feat (Kit Aluri):** Grants advantage on Investigation checks for traps and hidden features. DM acknowledged this was overlooked during S05 play and should be applied going forward.
- **Help action in combat movement:** DM allowed Kit to use help action to assist Binks's Athletics check for movement through difficult terrain (S08).
- **Jump rules (S10):** Long jump = distance equal to Strength score (with 10ft running start). High jump = 3 + Strength modifier (running) or just Strength modifier (standing). Jumping is part of movement, not an action.

### Spellcasting
- **Casting scrolls above your level (S09):** Per 2024 rules, casting a spell from a scroll above your level requires a spellcasting ability check (DC 10 + spell level). Binks successfully cast a 7th-level Resurrection scroll at Level 3 (DC 17). Guidance and Help action (from Blarg) were both allowed to apply to the check.

### Wild Shape (2024 Rules)
- **Moon Druid temp HP (S10):** Circle of the Moon druids gain temp HP equal to 3x their druid level when wild shaping (per 2024 rules). Blarg (Druid level 3) gets 9 temp HP. Wild shape no longer creates a separate HP pool — it adds temp HP to your existing pool.
- **Wild Shape does NOT recharge on short rest (S10):** Discussed at table — this was a 2014 rule. Under 2024 rules, wild shape charges do not return on short rest (Binks confirmed this at the table).
- **Goblin wild shape (S11, homebrew):** Blarg can use a wild shape charge to transform into a goblin. Keeps own stats but gains goblin appearance and Fury of the Small racial feature. Duration follows normal wild shape rules (1 hour per DM statement, though 2024 Moon Druid may differ — confirm).

### Homebrew Items & Features
- **Growth items (S11):** DM confirmed all empowered trinkets from the Feydark are "growth items" that improve at certain level thresholds. Track upgrades going forward. S13 pre-session confirmed stat bonuses: Kit +1 STR, Aeolus +1 WIS, Blarg +1 CHA, Darby +1 CHA (Axebeak form) and +1 INT (Pipe). Binks's trinket empowerment NOT included in Addison's review — gap.
- **Doll of the Fallen Soldier (S11, homebrew):** Kit's trinket. +1 CHA. On a failed death save, the doll animates: causes disadvantage on attacks against Kit (or chosen ally), carries target from danger. Cannot take attacks itself. Can be assigned to an ally instead of Kit. Uses "Sentient Armor" stat block per Addison (S13) — exact capabilities TBD.
- **Shillelagh on warpick (S11, homebrew):** Amanita can cast Shillelagh on her warpick (normally restricted to staves and clubs). DM ruling specific to her empowered warpick.
- **Rapport Spores upgrade (S11, homebrew):** Amanita's rapport spores now have unlimited range on the same plane after the initial 30ft emanation establishes the link. 3 uses per day, 1 hour each. Upgraded by Psilofyr.
- **Absent PC spellcasting by proxy (S12):** When Florian (Blarg) was absent, DM allowed present players to use Blarg's prepared spells (specifically Hold Person) as a surprise round action. Blarg's character was treated as present but on autopilot. Precedent for absent-player spell use.
- **Greatsword sweep in darkness (S12):** Kit ran through magical darkness with greatsword out. DM ruled a Dex save for the patrol drow she passed (who failed, took 9 damage). Not counted as an attack action — Kit retained her normal attack afterward.
- **Perception check to negate blindness penalty (S12):** DM allowed Kit a perception check (DC 15) to sense a nearby drow in magical darkness. Success = straight attack roll instead of disadvantage. Establishes precedent for fighting blind in darkness.
- **Rage maintenance via bonus action (2024 rules, S12):** Darby confirmed under 2024 rules, rage can be maintained via bonus action even without taking/dealing damage. DM accepted this.
- **Misty Step requires sight (S12):** DM enforced RAW: Misty Step requires seeing the destination. Cannot be used in magical darkness. Binks and Kit both blocked from using it while in Darkness spheres.
- **Athletics check to mount moving creature (S12):** Darby attempted to mount Roxy mid-charge. DM called for Athletics check with disadvantage (darkness), offset by rage advantage = flat roll. DC not stated; dirty 20 succeeded.

### Campaign Setting
- **Faerzress:** Ambient magical energy permeating the Underdark. Can be corrupted by chaotic/fiendish influence. Corrupted faerzress caused involuntary wererat transformation (S04), triggered short-term madness in Amanita when she cast Detect Magic on it (S05), and destabilizes the Feydark portal (S09). Connected to the corrupted entity's influence (S11).
- **No day/night cycle in the Underdark:** Long rest = 8 hours. Short rest = ~1 hour.
- **Feydark time is non-linear:** Temporally isolated from the Underdark clock (confirmed by DM and raven Echo in S10). ~2 days subjective Feydark time = ~1 hour Underdark time.
- **Archfey override (S10):** Psilofyr (Archfey of Myconids) overrode Constitution saving throws to force all PCs unconscious for a long rest. DM stated: "You're now dealing with an archfey." No save allowed. Precedent for archfey-level entities bypassing normal mechanics.
- **Short rest via Feydark time dilation (S10):** DM granted a short rest to all PCs based on subjective time experienced during the Feydark visions (~1 hour for most PCs). Kit was the only one who felt it lasted "only minutes" but was still granted the rest.
- **Amanita's symbiotic entity form in Feydark (S10):** DM allowed Amanita to enter symbiotic entity (Halo of Spores + temp HP) for free in the Feydark, as "being in this Feydark domain of the archfey of myconids, it just kind of fit."
- **Exhaustion at depth:** DM applied Exhausted condition to all PCs at end of S08 after ~6-8 hours travel without long rest. Asked for Con checks at short rest to potentially clear it.
- **Acid damage to armor (Black Pudding):** Black Pudding acid permanently reduces AC by 1 per hit. Armor destroyed if reduced to AC 10 (S08). Kit's armor took -1 AC.
- **Nexus Pools arrival timing (S11):** When the party chose a destination from the Nexus Pools, DM rolled a d20 to determine when they arrive. Scale: 0-5 = during demon attack; 5-10 = post-demons but drow still present; 10-15 = about a day later, quiet but defended; 15-20 = later, outpost mostly abandoned (drow chasing party). Roll: 10.
- **Nexus Pools are one-way (S11):** Psilofyr confirmed the party cannot return to the Feydark via the same portal. The Feydark realm shifts and the portals move. Faerzress corruption further disrupts portal stability.

- **S13: Jorlan Duskryn alive.** Previously listed as dead per Addison's 11/22/25 notes ("Older drow who helped the party and died"). Appeared alive in S13 — shot Ilvara with a crossbow and smiled. Either a retcon or the earlier note was misleading/in-world information rather than fact. Needs DM clarification.
