# House Rules & Rulings

DM Addison's rulings override all published rules. This page tracks confirmed homebrew and table decisions.

---

## Confirmed Rulings

### Combat Mechanics
- **Ranged spell attacks within 5ft:** DM waived disadvantage for S01 (Aeolus's Eldritch Blast vs spider). Enforced from S02 onward. Standard RAW applies going forward.
- **Wild Shape speech (2024 Rules):** Characters in beast form can speak normally. Discovered during S02 when Blarg wild shaped into wolf. This is per 2024 PHB rules, not homebrew.
- **Slashing damage vs. Black Pudding:** Confirmed S08 — slashing damage has no effect on Black Pudding (mass reforms). Bludgeoning and radiant confirmed effective. This triggered the pudding splitting into two halves after Kit's greatsword strike.
- **Friendly passage through Glabbagool:** DM ruled half acid damage (5 instead of 10) for allies passing through Glabbagool voluntarily (S08).
- **Trip Attack vs. oozes:** Battle Master Trip Attack maneuver can knock a Black Pudding prone (S08). Pudding failed Str save vs. Kit's DC.
- **Darby's mace damage upgrade:** DM upgraded Darby's mace extra damage from +1 flat to D4, and allowed radiant damage during S08 encounter (normally fire). DM may alternate between fire and radiant.

### Lycanthropy
- **Wererats have control over their transformations (homebrew):** DM corrected in S05 — wererats normally have much more control than werewolves over their shifts. Topsy and Turvy's transformation in S04 was involuntary and abnormal, caused by corrupted faerzress. This is a DM homebrew distinction.
- **Moon influence on wererats (homebrew):** Wererats are still affected by the moon per DM ruling (S05). The moon flask given to Turvy channels lunar energy to help control changes.

### Exploration & Skills
- **Dungeon Delver feat (Kit Aluri):** Grants advantage on Investigation checks for traps and hidden features. DM acknowledged this was overlooked during S05 play and should be applied going forward.
- **Help action in combat movement:** DM allowed Kit to use help action to assist Binks's Athletics check for movement through difficult terrain (S08).

### Campaign Setting
- **Faerzress:** Ambient magical energy permeating the Underdark. Can be corrupted by chaotic/fiendish influence. Corrupted faerzress caused involuntary wererat transformation (S04) and triggered short-term madness in Amanita when she cast Detect Magic on it (S05).
- **No day/night cycle in the Underdark:** Long rest = 8 hours. Short rest = ~1 hour.
- **Feydark time is non-linear:** Temporally isolated from the Underdark clock (confirmed by DM and raven Echo in S09).
- **Exhaustion at depth:** DM applied Exhausted condition to all PCs at end of S08 after ~6-8 hours travel without long rest. Asked for Con checks at short rest to potentially clear it.
- **Acid damage to armor (Black Pudding):** Black Pudding acid permanently reduces AC by 1 per hit. Armor destroyed if reduced to AC 10 (S08). Kit's armor took -1 AC.

### Retcons
- **S08: Exhaustion tracking retconned by Addison.** S03-S07 time/condition data should be treated as approximated. All PCs reset to 1 level of exhaustion at S08 start.
