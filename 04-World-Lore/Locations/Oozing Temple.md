# Oozing Temple

## Description

A subterranean temple located deep beneath the ancient Dwarvish/Duergar safe house. Accessed via a series of descending shafts, bone caverns, and acidic flooded tunnels. The temple features:

- **Seven wall niches** — One cracked and leaking water; two empty; four contained gray oozes disguised as "formless sculptures"
- **Central fountain** with raised stone edge containing shallow brackish water
- **Broken statue** on pedestal — Initially only clawed stone feet visible. As gray oozes were killed, their remains seeped into the stone and reformed the statue into a dragon shape. When the last ooze died, the statue's eyes lit up and the fountain basin opened to reveal treasure.
- **Carved reliefs** on walls showing worn, faded swirl shapes that might be waves, tentacles, or a combination thereof
- **Heavy flagstone flooring** — Four passageways leading into the temple had weakened flagstones concealing deep pits with black ooze below

## Notable Features

- **Dragon Statue:** Reformed from gray ooze remains during combat. Purpose and identity unknown. Eyes lit up when last ooze died. Possible connection to the Faceless Lord (Juiblex) or the demonic corruption affecting the Underdark.
- **Flooding:** Water seeping through wall cracks accelerated during/after combat. Temple was ankle-deep in rising water by end of S06. Flooding became critical in S07.
- **Tentacle/Wave Reliefs:** Worn carvings may indicate a connection to an ancient aquatic or aberrant deity.

## Treasure Found

From the fountain basin after the dragon statue reformed:
- 112 silver pieces
- 41 gold pieces
- 3 green gold bracelets (25 gp each)
- +1 Dagger (drow design, spider hilt)
- Potion of Greater Healing
- Oil of Slipperiness (vial)

## Encounters

- **Combat 3 (S06):** 4 gray oozes attacked from wall niches. Party defeated all. Ooze remains reformed into dragon statue.
- **Flooding escape (S07):** Environmental skill challenge as water rose rapidly.
