# The Feydark

> **Type:** Fey-touched Underdark region / Archfey domain
> **Ruler:** [[Psilofyr]] (the Carrion King, Archfey of Myconids)
> **Significance:** Major arc location (S10 onward). DM Addison's homebrew expansion — "The actual resource does not go into the Feydark at all."

---

## Description

A fae-touched subterranean realm within the Underdark. The Feydark is the domain of [[Psilofyr]], near the Court of Stars. Time is non-linear — approximately 2 days of subjective Feydark time equals approximately 1 hour of Underdark time.

### Environmental Details (S10)
- **Arrival:** Thick, soupy fog. Sweet rot and damp earth smell. Warm, humid air. Spongy lichen and mud underfoot. Underground but feels closer to the surface.
- **Visibility:** Heavily obscured for most PCs (Perception needed). Amanita (20) and Darby (19) were lightly obscured. Others heavily obscured/blinded.
- **Atmospheric effects:** Constitution saves required on arrival (all PCs saved; DC was low). Feydark air contains active spores.
- **Flora:** Massive root systems, autumnal leaves, ferns, dripping water, bioluminescent fungi, spongy lichen. Feydark-specific mushroom species not yet identified.
- **Fauna:** Myconid Sovereign + Adults (trial encounter). Fae goblins (allies). Roxanne instinctively recognized the path — may have lived here previously.
- **Sky:** Sunset glow visible. Moon visible. Not a true sky — underground vault with fae illumination.

### Visions
The Feydark creates personalized visions for visitors — deceased family members appear as myconid avatars. Visions include warnings about a "great evil" threatening the Underdark. Not all visitors receive visions (Kit did not).

---

## Key Locations Within the Feydark

- **Arrival Zone:** Fog-filled area immediately beyond the portal. Party separated into three groups.
- **Binks's Vision (Table):** Clearing with table, food, three familiar figures (grandfather + 2 brothers).
- **Darby's Vision (Farm):** Open pen with axe beaks, rock walls, roots, fungus, leaves.
- **Roxanne's Path:** Winding path at slight incline. Massive root systems, autumnal leaves, dripping water. Roxanne instinctively followed this route.
- **Cliffside Clearing:** Top of cliff overlooking a massive gorge. Rushing water below. Sunset glow + moon visible. Site of the Myconid Sovereign trial (S10).
- **Court of Stars:** Referenced by Echo as nearby. Not yet visited by the party.
