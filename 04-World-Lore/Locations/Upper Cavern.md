# Upper Cavern (NE Exit)

## Description

A dry cavern chamber connected to the ancient Dwarvish safe house network. The party emerged here through a sealed Dwarvish stone door after escaping the flooded tunnels. The cavern features dry ground, lichen on the walls, trickling water, and a visible stairway going down.

## Notable Features

- Sealed Dwarvish stone door (opened by party; ancient abjuration magic)
- Lichen and some bioluminescence on walls
- Stairway descending to the south/right
- [[Amanita de'Champignon]] sensed mycelium at a distance — something alive and vast ahead
