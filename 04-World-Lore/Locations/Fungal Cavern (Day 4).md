# Fungal Cavern (Day 4)

> **Region:** Underdark tunnels, en route from [[04-World-Lore/Locations/Velkynvelve]] to [[Sloobludop]]
> **First Visited:** [[SITL_15_050326_The_Long_Road_to_Sloobludop|Session 15]]
> **Discovered By:** [[Amanita de'Champignon]] (encounter roll of 4)

---

## Description

A large cavern filled with fungi and mushrooms of all sizes and types, encountered on Day 4 of the party's 7-day journey from Velkynvelve to Sloobludop. Groundwater trickles through the cavern, consistent with the general trend of increasing moisture as the party descends toward the Darklake region.

|---|---|---|---|

| Nightlight | General Underdark fungus | Low | Noted |
| [[Waterorb]] | Water-containing fungus | None | Party may have refilled waterskins |
